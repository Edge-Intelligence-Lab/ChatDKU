Student Activities and Clubs

Duke Kunshan University is committed to providing students with a variety of student activities and services, encouraging students to explore their interests outside of the classroom. Our student activities and services are divided into 4 sections: Student Clubs and Organizations, Campus Activities, Art and Culture and the Blue Oasis Student Learning Center. These activities aim to strengthen cross-cultural understanding, promote extracurricular learning, increase participation in community service, enhance peer interaction and communication, carry out artistic and cultural exploration, and promote students’ personal development. Whether you like sports and fitness, art and culture, or volunteering in the community, you can always find like-minded peers at Duke Kunshan to share in your interests.

Student clubs and organizations

Student clubs and organizations are an integral part of campus life at Duke Kunshan University. Not only do they meet the wide range of interests in our students, but they also allow students to closely connect with each other in their spare time. Student clubs and organizations provide students with opportunities for development in leadership, cross-cultural understandings, social skills, and cognitive skills. Currently, the school has more than 65 student clubs and organizations with focuses on academics, culture, sports and community service. Students are also encouraged to establish new clubs and organizations if they have a unique interest that they would like to share with the community.

In the fall of 2019, a “Campus Activity Board” was established to facilitate engagement among students. This student organization focuses on students’ needs, interests and school event participation, with a mission to build an inclusive community that meets the needs of students in social, cultural, artistic, and other areas.

Art and Culture

The art and culture series of activities provide students with opportunities to explore, learn, and discover the arts. Artists in the fields of painting, design, music, film, handicrafts, and photography are invited to conduct seminars and workshops, discussing their work and sharing their appreciation of art and culture with students.

Students can also participate in the monthly DKU ArtBus – a short-distance excursion to museums, theatres and more in the Shanghai/Jiangsu area, generally to watch seasonal exhibitions. Students have the opportunity to meet exhibition directors and artists to learn more about contemporary art in China and its development in the Shanghai/Jiangsu area.

The school also organizes a week-long music and art festival every year, which includes roundtable discussions, seminars, concerts, charity art exhibitions and other activities. The purpose of this festival is to encourage students to communicate and interact through various creative media while building a bridge between the school and the local community.

Campus Activities

At Duke Kunshan University, students have the freedom to participate in a range of weekly activities. These activities provide an excellent platform for cross-cultural communication, team building, and personal leadership development. Students can build and enhance long-lasting friendships while enjoying themselves in a relaxed environment.

Another exciting opportunity for students is to participate in a DKU Quest trip. Much more than a typical field trip, DKU Quest integrates global and traditional Chinese elements, providing opportunities for students to explore China’s cultural, historical and natural heritage. These unique trips allow the students to deepen their cross-cultural understanding while building long-lasting friendships.

Blue Oasis Student Learning Center

In order to provide students with an exclusive social space where they can relax and pursue their interests, Duke Kunshan University built a brand new student activity center - The Blue Oasis. Located in the "Dayu Bay" shopping area just beside campus, this student-only facility consists of a reading room, a music room, craft room, a game room, an amphitheater, a relaxation area, and a club activity area. All DKU students can enter with their ID card, and are free to find a corner to call their own!
