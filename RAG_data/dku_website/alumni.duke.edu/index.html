    A Duke Centennial Celebration in Northern California: Duke Alumni Go to Wine Country

    Jun 01 2024, 11:00am HST

    How do you bottle passion? What is “biodynamic agriculture?” And is there any place more beautiful than Sonoma in springtime?

    Join us at historic Buena Vista Winery for a tour and tasting with winery staff and Boisset Collection Wine Ambassador Derek Mims B.S.'98. We will also have lunch on-site, catered by the Oakville Grocery.   

    Learn more and register >

    Annual Open Board Meeting

    Jun 05 2024, 6:30pm EDT

    Duke alumni and current students are invited to meet the DukeDC Regional Board of Directors at our annual open meeting! Join us to learn more about the operations of DukeDC, including leadership and volunteer opportunities, affinity groups and ways to serve our Duke community. Join us to share your talents and suggestions. 

    Learn more and register >

    Guided Tour of The Kinsey African American Art & History Collection

    Jun 07 2024, 10:00am CDT

    Join Duke Houston for a guided tour of The Kinsey African American Art & History Collection, featured at the Holocaust Museum Houston. This extraordinary collection is the largest private holding of African American art and artifacts, and the collection celebrates the achievements and contributions of Black Americans from 1595 to present day.

    Learn more and register >

    LGBTQ+ and allies Pride Brunch

    Jun 08 2024, 12:00pm EDT

    Join us for the inaugural Duke Boston LGBTQ+ and Allies brunch to celebrate LGBTQ+ pride month as we aim to strengthen and expand our Duke Boston community! This fun event will be a great way to meet new and old friends from the Duke Boston community as we share in Duke’s vision of inclusivity!
