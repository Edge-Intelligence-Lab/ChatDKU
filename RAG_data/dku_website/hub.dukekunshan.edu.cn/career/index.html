FAQs

How do I apply for an administrative/staff position at Duke Kunshan University?

You may create an account on our job application portal and search and apply for the job you are interested in. The portal is updated daily and lists all non-faculty positions within the University. We encourage all candidates to carefully review our FAQ prior to applying for an open position at Duke Kunshan University.

I am interested in research positions (Postdoctoral Fellow, Research Specialist, and/or Scientist), how do I apply?

You may apply for a research position through portal. In the meanwhile, you are also encouraged to send application materials to the hiring faculty’s mailbox. You could find hiring faculty’s email address in job description. For more information about applying research positions, please refer to Here.

How do I apply for faculty positions at Duke Kunshan University?

Duke Academic Committee is responsible for the faculty recruitment. The detailed application information could be found in each position JD. There is no need to apply for the faculty position via the portal. You can find more faculty positions Here.

Can I receive feedback on my application?

Due to the high volume of applications submitted, we are unable to provide status updates for each applicant. You will be contacted in the event that you are selected for an interview. You can check your application status through the portal.

How many job openings can I apply for?

It is okay to apply for multiple jobs, but you are encouraged to do as much research as you can, and apply for the one that matches your career path and experience the most. Please clarify your top-priority and submit your application for that job.

I am currently a registered student and am looking for internship. Can I apply?

Yes, we are hiring Interns for various offices. If you are interested in having an internship at DKU, you can see more intern positions Here.

I am currently a registered student, can I apply for full-time jobs?

Yes, you can. If you are a graduating student, you can apply for either intern positions or full-time jobs at DKU. Please note that you will start as an intern first before you officially obtained the graduation certificate and degree.

Can I send resume and cover letter to email address to apply for a job?

The official application way is to submit your application through portal. Go register an account on the portal now.

How long will the recruitment process be?

It depends. There will be at least two rounds of interview, followed by the reference/background checking. Staff recruitment process is on a rolling base.

What is cover letter for?

The cover letter acts as a personal introduction and gives you the chance to provide additional information about skills and experiences related to the job you are applying to. You are highly suggested to submit a cover letter as the supportive application material.

I am currently an employee of Duke Kunshan University, and I am interested in jobs as an internal candidate. How can I apply?

Welcome to explore the internal jobs! To learn more about the eligibilities of being considered as an internal candidate, please visit here.

I am not ready to apply for a job at Duke Kunshan University now, but I would like to see if there is any job I may be interested in. What should I do?

You are encouraged to create an account on the Portal first. You may apply for the job whenever you are ready to.

What do I do if I forgot my password?

Please click on the “Forgot your password” button on the sign in page. You will be redirect to a page to reset your password.

Can I change my password?
Yes, please go to the “Password Management” section to change your password.
What should I do if I meet technical problems when applying position in the portal?

Please kindly contact us by dkurecruitment@dukekunshan.edu.cn.
