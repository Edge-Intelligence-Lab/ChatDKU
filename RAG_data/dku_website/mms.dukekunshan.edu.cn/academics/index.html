ACADEMICS

The Master of Management Studies: Duke Kunshan University program offers you the best of both worlds. The core curriculum is delivered in English by the Fuqua School of Business’ top-ranked faculty at Duke University in Durham, NC, as well as at Duke Kunshan University in Kunshan, China. At both locations, you’ll enjoy world-class educational facilities and a rigorous, engaging business education.

The program’s 10-month format gives you a firm foundation fast. The curriculum includes both the hard and soft skills needed to develop your career. You’ll gain a solid understanding of business, including accounting, finance, economics, marketing, strategy, operations, management, and decision science. You’ll build new professional capabilities, with coursework focused on the analytical, critical thinking, communication, and leadership skills that will make you stand out in any role.

Engage yourself in a global classroom. From the U.S. to China, the MMS: Duke Kunshan program will give you the opportunity to dive deep into the cultures of two economic giants. Through team-based assignments, a stimulating instructional design, and active experiential learning, we’ll train you to rethink the boundaries of global business. In a business world where cultures are increasingly intertwined, you must have true global understanding to make an impact.

The MMS: DKU program has prepared me for my career in international business, allowing me to experience first-hand both American and Chinese business cultures. With a global point of view and exceptional academic training, I now feel qualified to take my place in the world of international business.

— Lucia Garcia, Argentina
