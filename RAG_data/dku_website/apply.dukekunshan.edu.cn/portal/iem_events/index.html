About Duke Kunshan

    DKU Events for Prospective International Students

    Note: To register for any of these events, scroll to the bottom and select an available date from the calendar on the right

    We are pleased to offer the following ways you can connect with the DKU Community

    Student Experience Panel (Recorded)




    Please join a very special virtual panel of Duke Kunshan University students from around the world. At this session, you will have an opportunity to hear first-hand what it's like to be a student at DKU. At this live interactive panel, you will:

    • Hear from those who are living it, what it is really like to live and study in China
    • Learn about the various clubs and organizations our highly engaged students are involved in
    • Discover the abundant research, and other cool experiential on- and off-campus opportunities, available to students
    • Get your questions answered by DKU students with diverse interests and backgrounds
    • Ultimately, come away with a better understanding of DKU’s unique student culture and global community!

    You may be working on your application to DKU right now. If so, this panel is highly recommended. What you learn at this panel can definitely help you with your 'Why DKU' essay. If you are interested in hearing what it is like to have a truly international university experience, you won’t want to miss this special LIVE opportunity!

    This live event was held on December 9, 2023. This year's recording can be accessed here. To view last year’s recording, click here.


    LIVE Virtual Campus Visits

    Like a campus tour, but conducted online! Hosted by one of our engaging student ambassadors, this 45-minute live presentation will help you get a feel for the campus and student life at DKU as an international student. You will be encouraged to ask questions and interact with other attendees. The student ambassador will share their perspectives and experiences using visual aids and anecdotes, giving you the opportunity to:

    • See and hear about our amazing facilities on campus
    • Learn about life as a student at DKU
    • Learn about what our students do outside of campus, including their travels around China and beyond

    LIVE Virtual Information Sessions

    Would you like to learn details about the undergraduate program's unique curriculum? Wondering about admissions requirements and financial aid? Hosted by a DKU admissions officer, these live info sessions will help you gain in-depth insight into: 

    • Our unique academic model
    • The connection to Duke University
    • Our interdisciplinary majors
    • Costs and financial aid
    • How to apply

    Alumni Panel (Recorded)

    Interested in hearing from recent alumni share their insights into how DKU prepared them for their post-graduate journeys? You are invited to a special session of Duke Kunshan University recent graduates from around the world.

    At this live interactive panel, you will:

    • Discover how DKU's unique interdisciplinary liberal arts and science curriculum prepares you for a wide range of career paths
    • Learn about the collaborative community, various resources, and interactive academic experiences offered to help students find their passion
    • Hear from Alumni on how they've taken the next step in their career journey

    Whether it’s working in industries around the world or entering elite graduate programs, you will learn how DKU had prepared them for success as global leaders. Don't miss this opportunity to hear from DKU's Class of 2022. 

    While this live event has passed, you can access the recording here. 

    Not available for any of the scheduled virtual campus visits? Check out our 360 tour at any time!
