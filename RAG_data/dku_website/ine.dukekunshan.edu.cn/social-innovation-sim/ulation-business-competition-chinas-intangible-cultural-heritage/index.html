Social Innovation Simulation Business Competition – China’s Intangible Cultural Heritage

You are here:
  1. Home
  2. InE News
  3. Social Innovation Simulation Business Competition…
Apr182024
InE NewsFeatured News

“Social Innovation Simulation Business Competition” is a public interest social innovation youth activity hosted by G20YEA Future Innovators Center (CFI) in Duke Kunshan University. This event aims to lead young people to deeply explore the nature and value of social innovation through exchanges, discussions and practices, help them establish positive social values, cultivate innovative thinking and teamwork ability, and inspire their attention and action to global challenges. Social innovation is an important engine to promote inclusive global economic growth and social change. In this era of rapid development, CFI hopes to cultivate generations of young innovators to lead the world to a better direction. We believe that “Social Innovation Simulation Competition” will open a door to the collision of ideas, innovative exploration and common growth for the participating young people, and contribute new strength and wisdom to social change and sustainable development.

Event Info.

Topic: Social Innovation Simulation Business Competition – China’s Intangible Cultural Heritage

Time & Date: April 27, 10 AM-4:00 PM

Venue: AB Auditorium (Lunch Will be Provided)

Register

Guest Info.

Yichen Tao

Deputy Secretary-General of G20YEA Future Innovators Center & Social Innovation Mentor of Waston Institute

Sherry Zhang

Secretary-General of G20YEA Future Innovators Center & Hurun U30 China Entrepreneurship Pioneer

Event Agenda

10:00-10:10: Camp Opening Ceremony

10:10-11:40: Themed Lectures (Concept, Importance, and Applications of Social Innovation with Cases Analysis)

11:40-13:00: Lunch Break

13:00-14:00: Innovation in Action Business Simulation Competition (Grouping, Analysis of Intangible Cultural Heritage Social Innovation Cases, Propose Innovative Insights and Solutions)

14:00-15:00: Presentation and Sharing

15:00-15:15: Intermission

15:15-16:00: Camp Closing Ceremony

Activity Gains

  • Discover China’s Intangible Cultural Heritage in a Shifting Global Landscape
  • Analyze Intangible Cultural Heritage Social Innovation Cases and Propose Innovative Views and Ideas
  • Direct Dialogue with Professional Social Innovation Mentors to Expand Your Professional Network
  • Certificate of Social Innovation Simulation Competition
  • Opportunity to Learn about International Organizations and Become Interns and Youth Representatives of CFI

CFI Intro.

G20YEA Future Innovators Center (CFI): G20YEA Center for Future Innovators (G20YEA CFI) is a core youth growth project created by the G20YEA China Council for students. Through a variety of activities and projects, focusing on important issues related to G20, integrating and bringing together top academic mentors, leading outstanding young people in China to deeply explore current social hot spots and put forward innovative suggestions and solutions, aiming to drive more social forces to understand and participate in the development goal of inclusive global economic growth, and to promote the cultivation of international innovative youth.
