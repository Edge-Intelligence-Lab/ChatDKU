DKU Care Team Report

This CARE report may be used to share concerns about a student's behavior or health with the Office of Student Affairs. Expressing concern shortly after an issue is raised or incident has occurred can help with early intervention and success. When completing this form, please provide as much information as possible. While anonymous reports are permitted, we have found that interventions are more effective when the student understands the genesis of the report including the name of the person sharing the concern.


These reports will be reviewed throughout the day, during normal business hours. A staff member from CARE Team will acknowledge your concern when it is received if you include your name in the report, and may contact you for additional information.


If you have a question or concern or would like to speak with a staff member directly during business hours, please call 0512.3665.7034 or email dku-ocm@dukekunshan.edu.cn


IF YOU BELIEVE A STUDENT IS IN IMMEDIATE DANGER OF HARMING HIM/HERSELF OR OTHERS, PLEASE CALL THE 24-HOUR HOTLINE AT 0512.3665.7110, OR DIAL 110.


Thank you for caring about our students' success.
