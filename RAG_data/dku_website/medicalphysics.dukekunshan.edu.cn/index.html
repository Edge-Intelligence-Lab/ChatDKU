EARN A DUKE DEGREE

Students successfully completing the program will receive a Master of Science degree in medical physics from Duke University.

CURRICULUM

The Medical Physics Program at Duke Kunshan University (DKU) offers students a unique opportunity to study medical physics at a joint venture university with close affiliation to Duke University and Wuhan University and with distinguished faculty internationally experienced in the practice of medical physics.
Click Here

Comprehensive Medical Physics Training

Duke Kunshan’s MP program offers extensive training in three major medical physics tracks: Radiation therapy, diagnostic imaging, and nuclear medicine. Students can choose to increase emphasis in specific tracks through year-2 course selections and through choice of scholarship or thesis projects. Click here for additional information about the curriculum.

The program curriculum design follows the AAPM (American Association of Physicists in Medicine) guidelines and is SACS (Southern Association of Colleges and Schools) accredited. CAMPEP has not been accredited. Upon graduation, students will complete all of the courses required for applying for Ph.D. programs in medical physics in the U.S. and Canada, which is an innate advantage for our students in Ph.D. program application.

“Become a leader in the emerging field of medical physics, fusing your expertise in physics with improved medical care. This is a dynamic profession that applies the concepts of physics to the diagnosis and treatment of human disease, with career paths that include academic, research, and clinical settings.“
