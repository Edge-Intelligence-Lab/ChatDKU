6.03 Scope This Policy protects members of the DKU community from discrimination, harassment, relationship violence, stalking and related misconduct that occurs on DKU property (whether owned, leased, operated, or managed) or occurs while such members are participating in a DKU-sponsored or affiliated activity. It also applies to conduct that occurs off DKU property and not in the context of a DKU-sponsored or affiliated activity but has continuing adverse effects on a DKU-sponsored or affiliated activity.
