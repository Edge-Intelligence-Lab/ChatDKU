If a student will miss 2 or more consecutive instructional days due to illness, s/he should obtain official documentation from either Campus Health Services/Counseling and Psychological Services or from an external health care provider. The Campus Health Services/Counseling and Psychological Services will work with the Dean of Undergraduate Studies or designee to coordinate with the student’s instructors and academic advisor to support the student. Absences of a single day or less due to illness are generally handled directly between the student and faculty.
