Duke Kunshan University (hereafter DKU or the University) is dedicated to pursuing its missions of education and scholarship and furthering the search for wisdom. The University is committed to fostering a working environment that encourages unfettered inquiry and the free and open exchange of ideas. DKU seeks to create an environment that effectively supports the mission of the University, is free of behaviors that undermine the mission or the effective functioning of the University, and in which members of the University community are civil and respectful to others.
