Article 15. For projects with multiple collaborating institutions, each institution shall separately develop its budget plan according to its contribution to the research tasks. The principal (leading) institution of the project shall verify and organize the collaborators’ budget plans and compile into one overall budget. If DKU serves as the principal (leading) institution, the DKU Principal Investigator shall promote cooperation and careful organization to avoid duplication and redundancy in the budgets of the collaborating institutions.
