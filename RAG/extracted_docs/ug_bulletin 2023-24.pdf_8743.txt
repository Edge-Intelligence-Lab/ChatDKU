This is the first course in the beginning level German sequence. In this course students will build a solid base of vocabulary and pronunciation skills and set a foundation for reading, writing, listening and speaking in order to begin to engage in daily life in German. In this course students will also learn about how to learn a new language and about how to independently build language skills. This course will mostly be conducted in German and students will consistently and systematically use German to communicate both in and out of the classroom. There is no prerequisite.
