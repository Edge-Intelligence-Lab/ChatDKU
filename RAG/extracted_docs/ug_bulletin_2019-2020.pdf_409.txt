Withdrawing from a course is permitted in multiple fall/spring semesters, as long as a student maintains a course load of at least 16 credits. Withdrawing from a course to an underload (fewer than 16 credits) is generally permitted only once in a fall or spring semester. However, a student may begin another semester in an underload with certain restrictions (see below). A student may also be permitted to withdraw to an underload more than once if there are significant medical reasons (see below).
