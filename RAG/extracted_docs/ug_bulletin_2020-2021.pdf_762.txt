Student Affairs staff members are trained to maintain confidentiality of student information, student data and records, and disciplinary records (with limited exceptions such as when there is a crisis or crime being committed). The Clinic provides health education workshops to educate the campus community on handling emergency incidents, how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED), perform Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR), and apply First Aid. The clinic also provides seasonal flu immunizations.
