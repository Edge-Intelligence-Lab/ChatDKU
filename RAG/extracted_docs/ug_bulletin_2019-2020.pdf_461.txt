A student may not repeat a course after having passed another course for which the original course is a specific prerequisite. A student may not substitute a different course or a course taken at another institution for a repeat of a course taken at Duke Kunshan University. If a student repeats a course at another institution, the credits will not transfer; the previous DKU grade will not change and will remain in the student's GPA calculation. A course taken at another institution with a grade of C- or above and not transferred to Duke Kunshan University may not be repeated at Duke Kunshan University. If it is determined such a course has been taken elsewhere and repeated at Duke Kunshan University, the Duke Kunshan University course will be removed from the academic record. A course previously passed at another educational institution, however, may be audited at Duke Kunshan University.
