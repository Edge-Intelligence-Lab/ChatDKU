Aiming to promote and enhance an individual’s personal health and well-being by staying fit, resulting in a healthy campus, it also provides health education and promotion programs that help DKU community to acquire functional health knowledge, and strengthen their attitudes, beliefs, and practice skills needed to adopt and maintain healthy behaviors throughout their lives. Student Health Services has dedicated to creating an environment in which students feel empowered to make healthy choices and forming a caring community. The clinic also provides health education workshops to educate the campus community on handling emergency incidents, how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED), perform Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR), and apply First Aid.
