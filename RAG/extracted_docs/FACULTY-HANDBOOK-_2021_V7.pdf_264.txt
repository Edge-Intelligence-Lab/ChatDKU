According to the Duke Kunshan policy on Academic Tenure and Faculty Appointment, Promotion and Tenure, a tenure track faculty member may request an extension to his/her probationary period under exceptional circumstances. An ad-hoc supplemental policy, effective 11-5-2020, adds “disruptions to scholarship caused by the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020” to the list of exceptional circumstances and grants all tenure track faculty [who were full-time employees of DKU in 2020] an automatic tenure clock extension of one year, with an option to opt out.
