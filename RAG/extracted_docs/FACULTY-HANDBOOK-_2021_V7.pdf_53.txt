Under the supervision of the Chancellor, the Executive Vice-Chancellor sets the strategic direction of the University as well as manages the day-to-day academic affairs, curriculum development and general operations of the University in alignment with the world-class practices of Duke University. In performing his or her duties, the Duke Kunshan University Executive Vice-Chancellor, as appropriate, shall consult with the Chancellor and the Board of Trustees on significant matters. The Executive Vice-Chancellor is responsible for maintaining the University seal and its use, or authorizing its use.
