Duke University is a private research university located in Durham, North Carolina, United States. Duke offers 48 arts and sciences majors, four engineering majors, 50 Minors (including two in engineering) and Program II, which allows students to design their own interdisciplinary major in arts & sciences, and IDEAS, which allows students to design their own engineering major. Duke's student body consists of 6,582 undergraduates and 8,660 graduate and professional students (as of fall 2017).
