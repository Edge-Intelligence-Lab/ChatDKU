Throughout this Policy, the term "intellectual property rights" includes, inter alia, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, rights of publicity or privacy, moral rights, and all other neighboring rights of whatsoever kind; but the term excludes patent rights arising in inventions and supportive technology of the sort addressed in the University's Policy on Inventions, Patents and Technology Transfer (the "Patent Policy"). Know- how associated with patentable inventions or tangible material is not included in this Policy.
