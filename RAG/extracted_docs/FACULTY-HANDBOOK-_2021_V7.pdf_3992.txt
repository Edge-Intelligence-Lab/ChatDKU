Auditing division: the auditing division, titled the Audit Office, as charged by the University administration and the government, conducts internal audits of research funds on a regular, repeating basis and non-regular special audits of research funds as required. Internal Audit also conducts whole process tracking audits of Major and Key research projects, monitors project Principal Investigators and the University’s functional divisions for correct and reasonable use and management of the funds within each division’s authority. The division provides regular auditing reports to the University’s administration in a timely manner.
