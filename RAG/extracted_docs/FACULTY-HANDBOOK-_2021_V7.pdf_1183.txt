Kunshan has a number of local hospitals within close proximity to the Duke Kunshan University campus. Please note that most hospitals in China will not accept international medical insurance, with the exception of those clinics specializing in treating an international clientele. Western-style medical facilities with an international staff are available in Shanghai, Suzhou, and other large cities. Many other hospitals in major Chinese cities have VIP wards that feature up-to-date medical technology and physicians who are both knowledgeable and skilled. Most VIP wards also provide medical services to foreigners and have English- speaking doctors and nurses.
