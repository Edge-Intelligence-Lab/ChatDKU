This course reviews the history in which how China, as an ancient civilization, has revived from the fallen state in modern times to a mighty power in today’s world, helping students to understand and perceive Chinese society along with its changing trends with longer-term vision, the connotation of the Chinese national spirit, and the reason why today’s China cherishes independence while vigorously promoting opening up. This course requires class attendance and participation in discussion (4 credits), as well as a 7000-word themed paper (2 credits) in Chinese. Note that this is a compulsory course for all Chinese mainland and HMT students.
