Appropriate efforts will be made to protect the positions and reputations of those making allegations of misconduct, or providing related information, from any reprisals or retaliation unless those allegations or that information is judged to be baseless and malicious or reckless in nature. If, at any point in the misconduct review process, it is determined that the allegation or information was in fact baseless and malicious or reckless, the matter will be dealt with in accordance with existing university policies and mechanisms, e.g. the Harassment Policy or the Human Resources Work Rules.
