Return from any type of leave of absence, including suspension or an involuntary leave of absence, is contingent upon approval of a Return from Leave of Absence application. The deadlines to file such applications, including all supporting materials, are 5:00 p.m. May 1 (BJT) for Fall Semester and 5:00 p.m. October 15 (BJT) for Spring Semester. Late or incomplete applications will not be accepted. The University reserves the right to request necessary documentation and have conversations with appropriate parties, determined on a case-by-case basis, to confirm that the student is qualified and/or ready for return. Registration information will be provided by the university registrar to all students who are approved to return from a leave of absence. All returning students must register prior to the first day of classes for the term of intended enrollment. Students who fail to return as expected will be withdrawn from the university and will have to apply for readmission.
