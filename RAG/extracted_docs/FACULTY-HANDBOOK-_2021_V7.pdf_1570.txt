In the event of a permanent vacancy in the office of the Chair, the Vice-Chair of the Faculty Assembly shall serve as Acting Chair. The Acting Chair shall call a special election to elect a new Chair to serve the remaining period of the preceding Chair's term. Such special election shall be conducted at the earliest practicable regular or special meeting of the Faculty Assembly. The Faculty can vote to recall the Faculty Chair. This decision must be adopted by a two-thirds majority of votes and precipitates an immediate election for a new Faculty Chair.
