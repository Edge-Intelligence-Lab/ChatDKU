Bullying: Bullying and/or Cyber Bullying is defined as the process of intentional electronic, written, verbal, or physical acts directed at another student or students, or member(s) of the DKU community where there is intimidation or mistreatment that is severe, persistent, and/or pervasive and has the effect of interference with a student’s or students’, or member(s) of the DKU community’s educational environment, creates a threatening environment, or disrupts the orderly operation of DKU
