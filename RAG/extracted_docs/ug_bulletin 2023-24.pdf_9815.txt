This is American Sailing Association certified ASA 101 Basic Keelboat and 103 Basic Coastal Cruising combination course. It is provided over a five-day intensive coursework and is ideal for novices with minimal sailing skills and those who would like an in-depth refresher course and fast certification. At the end of the fast-track, students will have the skills necessary to safely and confidently operate and charter sailboats. Instruction will be held at Tai Lake, Suzhou, immediately after the final exams. There are supplemental charges for the course.
