This beginning level course aims to help students develop the basic Spanish skills for conducting common communication tasks such as talking about self and surroundings both independently and with other speakers. This course will also introduce students to the rich cultures of Spanish- speaking countries in Europe and in Latin America. Another integral component of this course is to guide students to gradually develop their own independent learning strategies for their potential future study of Spanish. The course will be primarily taught in Spanish and students are expected to not only follow the course but also contribute to a collective learning community by sharing their own independent learning materials and projects on a weekly basis.
