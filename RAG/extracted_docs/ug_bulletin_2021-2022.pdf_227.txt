Mini-Term courses are offered between the two sessions of the spring term. Students in the Class of 2022 are not required to take a mini-term course but may if desired. Students in the Class of 2023 and beyond are required to take one mini-term course in order to fulfill their degree requirement. Students may enroll in additional mini-term courses as space permits. Students can take these courses any time during their four-year study at Duke Kunshan University.
