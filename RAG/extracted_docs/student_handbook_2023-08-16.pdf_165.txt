4.02.02 General Student Alcohol Policy It is the policy of DKU to recognize the importance of the health and safety of the University community, including its students, especially as it relates to the use, possession, distribution, or consumption of alcohol (or other intoxicating substances). DKU expects its students will abide by policies and laws with respect to the use, possession and consumption of alcohol. With this purpose in mind, students must act responsibly and in accordance with this Alcohol Policy.
