This is an introductory French course in which students use French to communicate during class meetings and breakout sessions. The main objective is to develop the following intertwined proficiencies: linguistic and communicative skills (speaking, listening, reading, and writing), intercultural competence (knowledge, skills, and attitudes), as well as critical understanding of the French-speaking world in order to communicate in French (talk with others, express ideas in writing, read and understand what others have written or said).
