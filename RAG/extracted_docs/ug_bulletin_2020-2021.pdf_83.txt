Kunshan is culturally significant as the origin of Kunshan diao, the melody that ultimately evolved into Kunqu Opera, one of China's oldest extant theatre arts, which has been listed as UNESCO as World Intangible Culture Heritage. It has also been home to famous figures including Zu Chongzhi, Gu Yanwu, Gui Youguang, Gong Xian and Zhu Bailu.
