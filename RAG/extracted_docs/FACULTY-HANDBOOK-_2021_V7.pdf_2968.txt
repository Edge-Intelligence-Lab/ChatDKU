Intellectual property rights arising in courses approved for Duke Kunshan credits or Duke University credits but not developed via work-for-hire nor by affiliation with Duke Kunshan ordinarily belong to their individual creator in accordance with the general principles expressed in Article I.A. of this Policy; but rights may vest in Duke Kunshan to the extent that a course (or some portion of it) is created, acquired or developed by Duke Kunshan under Article I. B., or when the course (or some portion of it) falls within the exceptions set forth in Article II.
