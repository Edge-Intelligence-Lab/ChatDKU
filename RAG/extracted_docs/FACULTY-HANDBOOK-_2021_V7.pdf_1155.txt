Fitness classes, organized by Campus Student Activities of the Student Affairs Office, are also offered throughout the semester and open to faculty, with options such as yoga, Pilates, tai-chi, dance etc. Off-campus facilities at the Kunshan Sports Center (accessible by campus shuttle or public buses: #31, #32) offer discounted entrance fees to Duke Kunshan University students, staff and faculty for swimming, bowling, squash, badminton, tennis, basketball, etc.
