This office provides military training, mobility, Dang'An, Hukou, and train ticket discounts based on MOE's prescriptive procedures only for Chinese mainland students. Military training is essential for Chinese mainland students to learn at minimum military related theory, physical training, and field survival training. The office usually facilitates military training by the end of the first academic year. The office receives the Dang'An from the student's high school, files and keeps it. The file is kept in the office for four years and transfers to the place where students will go after their graduation. The office works with the local the Public Security Bureau (Police) to receive Hukou students transferred from their hometown to be registered as local resident although it's not mandatory. The MOE provides a discount for round-trip train tickets between home and DKU twice a year. The office purchases and places the discount sticker in the student's booklet to certify the discount.
