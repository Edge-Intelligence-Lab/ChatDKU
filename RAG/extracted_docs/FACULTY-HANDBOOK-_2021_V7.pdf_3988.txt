The research administration division: this division, titled the Research Support Office or RSO, is responsible for administration of sponsored research projects and programs (henceforth abbreviated as projects) including application preparation and approval, reviewing, negotiating and signing research contracts; and supervising the implementation and progress of each funded project. It advises principal investigators on preparing project budgets with assistance from the Finance Office; it verifies research fund types and project identification numbers; it reviews the financial reports prepared for closeout projects by the Finance Office for compliance with each 283
