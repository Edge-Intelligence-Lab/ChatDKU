The Indian Ocean world encompasses a vast geographical area from the East African coasts to the islands of South China Sea. As the oldest site of “globalization”, people and commodities moving across this ocean had played key roles in the spread of religion, highroads of commerce, and geo-politics of empire. Taking the period between 2500BCE and 1900CE as our point of reference we will study, the various approaches employed by historians towards writing the history of the Indian Ocean world. We will learn the various ways of thinking of Indian Ocean as a region and to periodize it.
