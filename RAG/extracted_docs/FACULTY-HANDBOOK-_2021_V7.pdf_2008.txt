The criteria for evaluating candidates for appointment, reappointment and promotion in regular, non-tenure track ranks as described in Appendix 2 and Appendix 3b (research and scholarship in the creative arts) will ensure comparable standards throughout the University. Candidates for reappointment or promotion will have access to these criteria prior to their review. Criteria should be more rigorous for each higher level of faculty rank and in general should be parallel to but not necessarily equivalent to those used for tenure track or tenured faculty. The Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs is responsible for ensuring equally rigorous criteria in different academic units. Appointment or promotion to the rank of Research Professor or Professor of the Practice requires outside review according to procedures that are determined by the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs.
