2.07.05 Quiet Hours Quiet Hours are in effect nightly Sun night – Fri morning from 11:00 PM – 8:00 AM, and Sat and Sun mornings from 1:00 AM – 10:00 AM. Reasonable levels of noise can occur outside Quiet Hours. Excessive or intrusive noise at any time is prohibited. 24-Hour Quiet Hours are in effect from the last day of class through the end of the session.
