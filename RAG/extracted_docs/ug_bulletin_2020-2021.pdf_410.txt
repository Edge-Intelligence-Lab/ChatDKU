Students who successfully complete a course at Duke Kunshan University for which they received precollege credit may not use that precollege credit or any higher precollege credit in that subject to satisfy degree requirements, but the precollege work will remain on the Duke Kunshan University transcript. A student who enrolls in a course for which precollege credit was awarded and who subsequently fails or withdraws from the course after the Drop/Add deadline will be allowed to apply the pre-college credit toward graduation requirements according to the policy governing use of such credits (see above).
