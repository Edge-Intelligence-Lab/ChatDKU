This course focuses primarily on realism (Howells, James, Gilman, Harper, Johnson), naturalism (Dreiser, Norris, Crane), and regionalism (Twain, Jewett, Chopin, Chesnutt) during the rise of consumer-managerial capitalism, first-wave feminism, and Jim-Crow Reconstruction. Possible attention to early utopian fiction (Adams, Bellamy, Gilman, Howells) or the impact of journalism (Dreiser, Crane, Henry Adams, Jane Addams, and the muckrakers) on fiction.
