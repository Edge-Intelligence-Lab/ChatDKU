Co-curricular Activities - are typically developed by students and advised by the Sr. Student Activities Officer. Students of clubs and organizations learn how to develop co-curricular activities for the entire Duke Kunshan University community including events that involve faculty and staff. Events include both on-campus programs and off-campus excursions. A range of activities offered each semester should appeal to the various interests of the Duke Kunshan University student population and support students’ academic, social, and physical wellbeing.
