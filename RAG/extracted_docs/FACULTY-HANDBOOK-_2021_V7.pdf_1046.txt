No matter when an accommodation is sought, the University will work in good faith with the employee requesting the accommodation as part of something called the “interactive process.” The process should begin with a written request by the employee or applicant submitted to the Human Resources Office. Once we have received your request, the University will evaluate the request in consultation with you and others as needed to determine what (if any) accommodation will enable you to perform the essential functions of the job. No accommodation will be provided that imposes an undue hardship on the University or poses a direct threat to the health or safety of the University’s employees, customers or vendors.
