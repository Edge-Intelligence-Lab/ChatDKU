A decision that an investigation is warranted is made by the Duke Kunshan University Executive Vice Chancellor upon his or her review of the SCMR’s inquiry into the allegation or as a result of an appeal by the complainant(s) of the Standing Committee’s finding. If the decision is to proceed with an investigation, the Executive Vice Chancellor will appoint an ad hoc committee to determine whether misconduct occurred. Additionally, a legal advisor shall be appointed to serve the ad hoc committee. Formation of the ad hoc committee will be governed by two principles: first, the need for impartiality on the part of the committee members, and second, the need for specific knowledge of the research field. The ad hoc committee will consist of at least three members selected to ensure that the investigation is carried out as completely and competently as possible. The ad hoc committee may include senior professors and external experts with knowledge of the research field of the individual suspected of misconduct and/or persons with expertise in other areas as necessitated by the nature of the research field or by the nature of the allegations.
