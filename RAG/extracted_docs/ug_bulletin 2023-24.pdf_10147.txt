The overall goal of this course is to develop and enhance the creative potential of the students and assist them to engage in innovation-driven activities through hands-on projects. The design of the course focuses on the core principles of project-based learning, creative thinking, and interdisciplinary collaboration. First, students will gain fundamental knowledge regarding the concepts and processes of creativity and innovation. Second, students will learn various tools to unlock their creativity, improve the flexibility and originality of their ideas, collaborate and contribute to a creative team. And third, students will practice what they have learned via inspiring exercises and projects on both an individual and group level.
