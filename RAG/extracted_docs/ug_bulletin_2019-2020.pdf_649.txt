The Office of Study Abroad is responsible for vetting and approving all academic study programs undertaken for credit abroad or off campus. The Office also handles course credit transfer requests for students studying in such programs. Students may study away and transfer credits for one regular semester in their junior year, as well as during summers. Students and their families are responsible for covering all costs relating to summer programs, including Duke University’s summer program. Students may transfer up to a maximum of 40 credits taken outside of the Duke Kunshan University campus, including advanced placement credits. (See Transfer of Work Taken Elsewhere, Part 6.)
