Such a partnership is particularly important during the period of transition and adjustment that typically takes place during the first-year in college. To ensure continuity of connection and progress toward graduation, the primary advisor will be with the student for the entire duration of their studies at Duke Kunshan University. Once a student selects an interdisciplinary community or disciplinary area of study, a second advisor (i.e. mentor) will be assigned to guide the student through his/her disciplinary and interdisciplinary studies and signature project.
