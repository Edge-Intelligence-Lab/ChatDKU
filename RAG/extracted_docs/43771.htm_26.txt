Yuan Yufeng has been selected for the Chinese Ministry of Education’s Talent Program and recognized as a young and mid-career expert who has made outstanding contributions to national health. He has long been committed to basic and clinical research on the pathogenesis and minimally invasive diagnosis and treatment of hepatobiliary and pancreatic diseases. He has been awarded honors, such as the National Award for Excellence in Innovation, the National ‘May 1st Labor Medal’ and the Hubei Province ‘May 1st Labor Medal’.
