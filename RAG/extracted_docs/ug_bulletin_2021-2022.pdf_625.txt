Students on academic probation must clear their probationary status as described above (see section on Academic Probation) in order to continue at Duke Kunshan University. For the purposes of clearing academic probation, incomplete work in any course is considered a failure to achieve satisfactory performance in that course. Therefore, where term continuation is in question, incomplete work in any course must be completed with a passing grade in time for final grades to be submitted to the Office of the University Registrar no later than the weekday preceding the first day of classes of the subsequent fall or spring term.
