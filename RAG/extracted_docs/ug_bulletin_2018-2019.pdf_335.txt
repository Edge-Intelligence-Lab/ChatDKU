Entering students who attended high schools where English was the medium of instruction will normally be assigned to the CSL track. During orientation at Duke Kunshan University, the Chinese skills of CSL track students will be assessed through a placement process that includes an interview, and they will be assigned to appropriate Chinese courses based on the results. In cases where students assigned to the CSL track feel they should be assigned to another track, they may make a request in writing to the Director of the Language and Culture Center.
