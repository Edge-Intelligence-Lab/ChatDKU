For courses taken at Duke University: All grades earned at Duke University are entered on the official record at Duke Kunshan University and are used in determining the grade point average. Courses taken while a DKU student is studying away at Duke University may be taken on a Duke Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory basis, subject to Duke’s processes, regulations and deadlines – which may differ from those at DKU. Such courses will also be subject to all regulations and limitations stated for the DKU CR/NC policy in the DKU Bulletin, including all restrictions on the number of credits and which courses can be taken on this basis. Students declaring S/U at Duke are required to consult their DKU academic advisor to ensure that they will be using a DKU CR/NC option in an appropriate manner. Courses taken at Duke on a S/U basis will transfer back to DKU and be recorded using DKU’s CR/NC system. Students who use S/U on a Duke course that cannot be transferred over as CR/NC at DKU will not earn credits for the course and may be ineligible to retake it at either institution.
