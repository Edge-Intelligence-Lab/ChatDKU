Probationary status cannot be cleared in a semester in which students seek permission and are allowed to withdraw to an underload. In such cases, the probationary status continues through the next semester of enrollment. Students who clear their probationary status by earning a C average in the semester but also earn grades that would result in academic probation as described above will continue their probationary status through the next semester of enrollment. Students on probation, whether in a normal load or an underload, are required to meet continuation requirements. Students whose probationary status continues to a second semester for reason of an underload or semester grades must adhere to the conditions and standards previously outlined for clearing probation. Failure to do so will result in academic suspension.
