“Individual” means an investigator, project director, co-investigator, and any other person regardless of title (and her/his immediate family) who is responsible for the design, conduct, or reporting of research, regardless of funding source; who teaches; or who makes or otherwise influences purchasing or contracting decisions. The principal investigator is responsible for identifying others who work with him/her who are responsible for the design, conduct, and reporting of the research for which extramural funding is sought or received.
