Undergraduate students will be assigned a faculty advisor. The faculty advisor will schedule times to meet with the student and is expected to meet with his or her advisees every semester; advisor approval is required for course registration. Advisors may also be asked at the behest of another faculty to consult with an advisee if the student is acting in a disruptive manner in that faculty’s course or if a student is performing poorly, in which case the advisor can help ascertain if intervention (such as tutoring or counseling) is required, or if a student is considering taking less than or more than the standard course load.
