Article 23. Tangible and intangible assets purchased for or created by a project supported by Domestic Government Funds are public property and managed by DKU; tangible and intangible assets purchased or created by a project supported by a Domestic Non-Government Funds and other research awards are DKU assets and shall be owned and managed by DKU.
