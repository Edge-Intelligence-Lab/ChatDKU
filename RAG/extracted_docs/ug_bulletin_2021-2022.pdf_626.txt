Students who fail to meet the minimum requirements to clear their probationary status are subject to academic suspension for a period of at least two terms (excluding summer term). Those desiring to return after the suspension period may apply for Return to the University. Such students are placed on academic probation if reinstated. Please contact the Office of Undergraduate Studies for more information. Deadlines for filing this application are 5:00 p.m. China time May 1 for return in the subsequent Fall Term and October 15 for return in the subsequent Spring Term. If after return the student fails again to meet the minimum academic requirements and is suspended a second time, the student will be ineligible to enroll again in the future.
