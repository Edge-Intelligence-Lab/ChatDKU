Once recorded, a notation of the I will remain permanently on the student’s record, even after the final grade is assigned for the course. In addition, an I cancels eligibility for Dean’s List and Dean’s List with Distinction in the semester in which the I grade was issued. Students who receive an I during a semester in which they experience a voluntarily or involuntarily separation, withdrawal, or leave of absence, from Duke Kunshan, may, at the discretion of their instructor, and the Dean of Undergraduate Studies, be required to complete the outstanding work prior to returning to school.
