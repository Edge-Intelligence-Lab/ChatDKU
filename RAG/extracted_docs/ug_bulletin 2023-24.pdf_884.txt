Counseling and Psychological Services is an important part of the Duke Kunshan University community, In addition to individual counseling, CAPS provides services to help students cultivate resilience and psychological flexibility and aims to create a healthy community through various community mental health engagement activities. CAPS places deep value in outreach programming and community engagement through various learning series on topics such as diversity, self-identity, self-confidence, how to form healthy intimate relationship, and how to live a meaningful college life.
