The co-curricular experiential learning component (e.g. internships, practica, community-based fieldwork or other civic projects) must comprise no fewer than 150 hours of work, and will be reflected on the transcript as non-credit, Practice-Oriented Education (POE). The signature product will vary across fields and disciplines, but will always include substantial writing, reflection on learning, and publicly visible results. A student's pathway will be developed by the end of the sophomore, or beginning of the junior year, at the latest. In the senior year, a student will create an e-portfolio that captures both the signature product a student has produced and a narrative explaining the larger inquiry informing their pathway.
