Simple FCOIs are managed by issuing a standard management plan to the Individual with the FCOI. A standard management plan will require that the Individual take certain steps, which may include but are not limited to one or more of the following: (1) follow-up with the IRB, IACUC, and/or Research Support Office; and (2) disclosure of the FCOI to potential research participants, academic journals and manuscript reviewers, audiences at conferences and seminars, and sponsors; and (3) recusal from directly influencing purchasing decisions. Standard management plans, which are issued by the Research Support Office, are reported and reviewed by the Conflict of Interest Committee at convened meetings. The Conflict of Interest Committee may accept or modify these plans as necessary.
