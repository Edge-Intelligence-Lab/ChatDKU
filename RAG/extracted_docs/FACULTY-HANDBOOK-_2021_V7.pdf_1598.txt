4.2.4.1 All Faculty Assembly Committees keep and distribute minutes of meetings to Faculty Assembly Committee members. Copies of the minutes shall be made available in a timely manner and saved electronically on a platform accessible to all members of the Faculty. No minutes, data, judgments, or other similar work product of Faculty Assembly Committees, nor any other account of meetings of the Faculty Assembly Committees, shall be disclosed or distributed, except to the Agenda Committee, the Faculty Assembly when assembled and its individual members, and by saving them to the aforementioned electronic platform accessible to all Faculty members.
