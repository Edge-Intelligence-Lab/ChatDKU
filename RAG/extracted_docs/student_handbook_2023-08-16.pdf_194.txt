Office of Student Conduct 4.10 Emergency Payment on Student’s Behalf A student in a medical or mental health emergency/crisis assumes all personal costs associated with the incident. It is conceivable that the University may incur a financial cost responding to a student in a medical or mental health emergency. A student who receives temporary financial assistance during a medical or psychological emergency or crisis is expected to pay back the University immediately. The amount related to assisting and/or triaging the incident will be billed to the student’s Bursar account. Unpaid balances may prohibit class registration and lead to being administratively withdrawn from the University.
