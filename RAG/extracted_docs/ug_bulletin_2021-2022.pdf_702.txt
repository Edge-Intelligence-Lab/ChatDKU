Academic advising is salient to undergraduate education at Duke Kunshan University. The goal of all academic advising is to provide a network of support across the university that is student- centered and responsive to the needs of students. Advisors provide insight on how to navigate the college experience by teaching students about the undergraduate curriculum and co-curricular opportunities. This process includes teaching students how each course contributes to their knowledge and innovative thinking. Additionally, advisors nurture students’ practical skills related to decision-making and help students learn to take responsibility for developing meaningful educational plans compatible with their academic potential, career, and life goals.
