A Duke Kunshan University student may receive credit for a maximum of 40 credits taken elsewhere, through a combination of AP/IPC credit or approved transfer or study away credit. Of those 40 credits, a maximum of 8 may be from AP/IPC/prematriculation credits (see section on Entrance Credit and Placement) prior to matriculation, and a maximum of 8 may be from coursework undertaken while on voluntary leave of absence for personal or medical reasons. No credit will be accepted for coursework taken while a Duke Kunshan University student is withdrawn, suspended, or on involuntary leave of absence.
