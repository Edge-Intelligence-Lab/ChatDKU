5.04.02 Residence Life Coordinator Staff Non-academic policy violation allegations, which are non-egregious, and occur within or adjacent to residence halls, and by Respondents without an existing conduct record will generally be adjudicated by Residence Life Coordinator staff. If staff determine, throughout the course of their investigation, that the nature of the incident is more appropriate for another Adjudication Body, they will make such a referral and notify the students involved.
