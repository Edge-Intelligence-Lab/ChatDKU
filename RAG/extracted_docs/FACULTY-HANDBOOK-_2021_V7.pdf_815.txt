A faculty member with main responsibilities in teaching or research, certified as a tax residence of a foreign country which has double tax treaty with China, may be eligible for China individual income tax exemption for 2 or 3 years. During the period of exemption, all of the compensation and benefits from DKU in China or Duke are free of individual income tax. The tax consulting firm will help faculty members to assess their exemption eligibility and apply for the exemption, if applicable.
