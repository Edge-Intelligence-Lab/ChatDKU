TRP is equivalent to a multiple-entry visa which can be obtained by going to the Kunshan Municipal Administrative Services Center with all the requisite documents and fees. Your will receive a receipt from the Kunshan Municipal Administrative Services Center. Be sure to keep this receipt in a safe location. Temporary Residence Permit (or Visa Extension) will take 15 working days to process before your passport is returned to you. If you need to travel within China, the receipt issued to you may be used in lieu of your passport for hotel registration and similar purposes. You will be unable to travel outside China within these 15 days. A TRP application fee is about 800 RMB. You can use Bank Card or Wechat Pay to pay for that. You need to pay attention: 1、 Health Check is required after China for TRP application. There will be six items, including Medicine, Surgery, Electronic Cardiogram, Ultrasonic, X-ray on your Chest, and Blood checking. For X ray Chest, if you have already done it in 3 months before you come to China, you need to bring the photo so that you do not need to do it again.
