This is an introductory academic writing course designed for international students who attended high schools where English was not the medium of instruction, and have not previously studied academic writing in English. It is designed to help equip students with the academic reading & writing skills in English that they need to succeed in their DKU courses. This content-based language course is focused on the topic of language learning motivation. Through academic reading, writing papers, and delivering presentations which explore theories and narratives on language learning, students will become well prepared to meet the academic expectations of their DKU professors.
