The color used in the logo inherits blue from Duke University, green from Wuhan University and the green from Kun Jade, which implies that the Duke Kunshan University is an open integration platform for the community to make contributions and foster more outstanding talents. And on the whole, the brand logo likes a crest of green mountains, echoes with Chinese philosophy of “the benevolent find joys in mountain”, shows the ideal of Duke Kunshan University: a commitment to the harmony of people and nature.
