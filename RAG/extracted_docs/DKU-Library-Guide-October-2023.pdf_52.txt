The Library provides one-on-one and group research consultations, in-class training sessions, and a series of workshops to teach you how to ﬁnd, evaluate, organize, and analyze information and data. For advanced research support, professional librarians provide consultations of systematic reviews and design research & course guides in various disciplines. The Library also provides access and instructional support for citation management tools such as Zotero and EndNote, and for data analysis and visualization tools such as R, ArcGIS, NVivo, and Tableau. Contact librarians, visit the Library website, or follow the Library on WeChat for more information.
