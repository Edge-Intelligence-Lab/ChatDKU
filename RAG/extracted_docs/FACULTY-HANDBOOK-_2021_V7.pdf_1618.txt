4.2.5.4 The Teaching and Advising Committee is responsible for making recommendations to improve the Faculty as teachers and advisors. To that end, it works collaboratively with the Center for Teaching and Learning and the Office of Undergraduate Advising to assist in the development of efforts to enhance Faculty capacity in these areas. Teaching and Advising Committee membership includes: five elected Faculty members from a minimum of five academic divisions and the Associate Vice-Chancellor for Undergraduate Programs (ex officio).
