The Office of Undergraduate Advising is committed to providing a student-centered advising integrated and experience that promotes holistic interdisciplinary curriculum and prepares the next generation of scholars and leaders. Further, academic advising provides undergraduates with academic strategies that reinforce a research- oriented liberal arts and sciences education that is inclusive of how students learn and develop in pursuit of their academic and career endeavors. The Office of Undergraduate Advising consists of Academic Advising and the Academic Resource Center.
