This is a guided independent study course in which learners will learn basic skills in the target language. The specific goals and other aspects of the course (e.g. materials, learning methods) to be determined through discussion between the learner and instructor, but normally learners in this course will learn how to introduce themselves, manage daily interactions, and deal with daily life issues; in other words, the course will cover the amount and type of material normal for a first term language course. The target language must be one which faculty in the Language Learning Studio are able to speak and can provide coaching for. This course is for students whose Third Language track students, i.e. those whose English and Chinese language proficiency is such that they need no further study of these languages.
