One option is to take independent study courses in a third language (TLANG) through the Writing and Language Studio. Another option is to take advanced written and oral communication (WOC) courses. Because the content of different sections of some WOC courses differ considerably, in some cases students are allowed to take more than one WOC course of the same course number if the course content of the two courses is different.
