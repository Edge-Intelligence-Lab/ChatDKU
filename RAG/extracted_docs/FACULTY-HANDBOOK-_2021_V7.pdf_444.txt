Faculty members (including part-time faculty) teaching in the undergraduate and graduate programs will be available in their offices at least four hours per week for each four-credit 7-week semester course and two hours per week for a 14 week semester course or a two-credit 7-week course, and by appointment at mutually convenient times. If unable to keep the posted times, a faculty member will post a note to that effect. For the Master of Management Studies program, in which each course is taught in an intensive 2-week format, faculty are expected to be available for student consultation outside of their time in the classroom during the two weeks in which they are in residence at DKU.
