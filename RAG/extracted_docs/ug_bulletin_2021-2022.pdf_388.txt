Violations of academic integrity that occur while the student is residing at Duke University or other institution (for example, during a study-abroad program) will be handled by the host institution according to the host institution’s policies, although DKU reserves the option to investigate the case and impose additional penalties if such action is deemed warranted. In the event a DKU student is suspected of an academic integrity violation at Duke University, DKU may share information regarding previous academic integrity violations that occurred at DKU. Duke University, at its discretion, may use this information in the resolution of disciplinary proceedings.
