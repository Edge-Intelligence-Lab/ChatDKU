Students who are in good standing with Duke Kunshan University will have the opportunity to attend Duke University in Durham, North Carolina, in either the fall or the spring semester of junior year. To the extent possible, the Office of Study Abroad will take into account individual timing preferences. Students will be able to choose from a wide range of course options. In addition to spending one semester at Duke University in their junior year, students may also opt to study at Duke University for one six-week summer term.
