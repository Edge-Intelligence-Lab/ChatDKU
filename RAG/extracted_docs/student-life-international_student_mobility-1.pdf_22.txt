You need to go for residence address registration in 24 hours after you enter China. If you live in a hotel, the hotel will do it for you. If you go directly on campus, Student Affairs Team will help you do that. But if you come to China earlier before the Check-in day, and you do not live in a hotel, such as living in your friend’s apartment, etc., you need to do it yourself. The basic procedure is to bring your passport to the nearest police station to your accommodation place. Visa Extension If the number of days of your academic program is longer than the duration allowed by your visa, you must apply for a “visa extension” in order to continue to stay in China. For visa extension (VE), the application fee is 140 RMB. Temporary Resident Permit (TRP)
