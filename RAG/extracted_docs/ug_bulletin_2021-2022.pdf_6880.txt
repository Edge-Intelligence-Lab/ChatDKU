Appreciation of World Chinese Literature Classics is a course that explores the beauty of world Chinese literature and the general rules of people's feelings about its beauty. On the one hand, this course starts with the basic concepts and logical structure of the world Chinese literature classics, and explores their significance in the generation of the language style and discourse system of the world Chinese literature in the 20th century; on the other hand, it illustrates the types, characteristics and effect on its unique contribution to the aesthetic construction and artistic presentation of Chinese literature, revealing its status and influence in the history of world literature.
