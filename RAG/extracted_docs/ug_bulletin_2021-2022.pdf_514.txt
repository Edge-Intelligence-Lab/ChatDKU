Students who have declared a major and wish to change it may do so by submitting a form to the Office of the University Registrar. No changes in majors can be made after the end of the Drop/Add period of the first session of the spring term in the senior year. Students receive three confirmations of their graduation: a Duke University diploma (issued by Duke University), a Duke Kunshan University diploma (from the Ministry of Education of the PRC), and a Duke Kunshan University graduation certificate (from the Ministry of Education of the PRC).
