Duke Kunshan University is a Sino-American partnership of Duke University, Wuhan University and Kunshan city in Jiangsu province to create a world-class liberal arts and research university offering a range of academic programs for students from China and throughout the world. A nonprofit, joint-venture institution, Duke Kunshan University was granted the accreditation approval by China’s Ministry of Education in September 2013.
