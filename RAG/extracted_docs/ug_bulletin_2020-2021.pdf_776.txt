Duke Kunshan University expects students to hold themselves to the highest standards for honesty, integrity, fairness, and responsibility in their academic and co-curricular endeavors. All students are expected to follow all university rules, policies, procedures, and processes. While there are various avenues for this, student need to familiarize themselves with the Student Code of Conduct, which can be found at: https://dukekunshan.edu.cn/en/student-life/student-handbook.
