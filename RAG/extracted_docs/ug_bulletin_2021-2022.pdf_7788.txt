Soundtracks, in their interaction with images, are decisive in shaping the narrative of movies. In this course, we will explore uses and functions of music and sound in film, with particular attention to the human voice. We will watch and analyze a wide range of movies and engage with the main aspects of the theory of film sound and its concepts. No prior musical knowledge is required. You will gain a better understanding of how sound helps determining the expressive messages of films. You will also learn to discern different styles and usages of sound in connection with images, and to write about these matters using specific terminology.
