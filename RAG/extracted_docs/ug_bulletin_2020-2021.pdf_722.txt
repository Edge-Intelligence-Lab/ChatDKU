Room assignments are made by Residence Life, before the school year begins, through the residence management system (RMS). Within the room assignment application, information about roommate matching, building preferences, and room type preferences will be collected, and students will sign a Room Assignment Agreement. While we collect these preferences, we ask for your understanding that there are limitations that don’t enable us to give everyone exactly what they prefer.
