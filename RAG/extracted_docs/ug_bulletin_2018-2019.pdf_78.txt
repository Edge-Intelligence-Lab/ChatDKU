Kunshan is culturally significant as the origin of Kunshan diao, the melody which ultimately evolved into Kunqu Opera, one of China's eldest extant theatre arts, which has been listed as one of the World Intangible Culture Heritages by UNESCO. In history, famous figures include Zu Chongzhi, Gu Yanwu, Gui Youguang, Gong Xian, and Zhu Bailu.
