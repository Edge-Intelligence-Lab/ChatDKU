Yuan Yufeng, male, of Han nationality, was born in May 1971. He holds a doctoral degree. He is a member of the Communist Party of China, a professor, chief physician, and doctoral supervisor. He is currently a member of the Party Committee and vice president of Wuhan University, where he assists the president in managing international exchanges and affairs related to Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan, international education, procurement and bidding, and medical education. He is in charge of the Office of International Affairs (Office for Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan Affairs), the Procurement and Bidding Management Center, the School of Medicine, the School of International Education, and the Hospital of Wuhan University. He is the contact person for the following schools: the College of Life Sciences, Taikang Medical School (School of Basic Medical Sciences), and The Second Clinical School (Zhongnan Hospital of Wuhan University).
