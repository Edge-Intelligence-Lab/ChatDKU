For students from the Chinese mainland, the credit-bearing requirements specified by the education administration authorities of China are: (1) Chinese Society and Culture courses (16 credits in total); (2) Military training (2 credits); (3) Eight half-credit physical education courses (4 credits in total, 2 of which can count toward the 136 credits for the Duke degree), and passing the physical proficiency test set by Ministry of Education of China. The total required credits is 156, inclusive of the 136 credits required for all students.
