Generally, the Faculty Assembly shall convene monthly at dates, times and places specified by the Agenda Committee during the regular academic year. The first meeting, at which elections to open seats are to be held, should occur in August, before the end of the first week of class. The date and time of meetings of the Faculty Assembly should ensure the largest possible participation from Faculty members. The Chair of the Faculty may cancel or postpone regularly scheduled meetings for lack of business or emergency circumstances. The Faculty Assembly shall also meet at the request of the Chancellor, the EVC or the VCAA, or upon the written request of 10% or more of the voting members of the Faculty Assembly.
