Duke Kunshan University will bear the financial burden resulting from a termination in the event only a specific program is affected. The two partners, Duke University and Kunshan municipal government, will bear the financial burden resulting from a termination due to the closing of the entire University. Tenure-track faculty on term appointments who have not been awarded tenure will be treated consistent with other faculty on term appointments and given notice of the early termination of their appointment consistent with Chinese law in the event of a change in an academic program or its elimination, and in the case that reassignment to another position is not feasible.
