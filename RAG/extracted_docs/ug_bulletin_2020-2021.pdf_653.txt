In recognition that observances of religious holidays may affect classroom attendance and the submission of graded work, members of the various religious affiliations should inform their instructor of major religious holidays that occur when classes are being held at or before the beginning of each semester. In the event of a conflict, students wishing to observe a specific religious holiday should request that their instructors arrange for a postponement or makeup of work. Duke Kunshan University encourages all faculty to make reasonable accommodations necessary for students’ religious observances. If an instructor is unable or unwilling to grant the request, students should consult the Office of Undergraduate Studies.
