4.12 Falsification/Fraud Any fabricated or fraudulent act that interrupts or interferes with the academic, co-curricular, or enrollment functions of DKU. This includes, but is not limited to, providing false information, using distortions, misrepresenting of information, forgery, alteration or misuse of University documents such as an ID card, access card key, an unauthorized duplicate of a key, or identification instrument and misrepresenting oneself to be an authorized official of the University or its auxiliary services.
