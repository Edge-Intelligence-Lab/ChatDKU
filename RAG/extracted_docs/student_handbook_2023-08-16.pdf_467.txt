Next Step: The complaint is forwarded to the staff supervisor to address the issue with the staff member • Resolution or Outcome: The staff supervisor requests a meeting with the student making the complaint. The staff supervisor will then have a meeting with the staff member to address the general issue of offering alcohol to students, which is prohibited to ensure that students’ wishes not to drink are respected. This is done confidentially without disclosing student names. The staff supervisor emails the student who filed the complaint to inform them that it has been addressed and to encourage the student to let the university know if it occurs again
