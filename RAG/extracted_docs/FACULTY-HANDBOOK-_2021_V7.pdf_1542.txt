For this to occur, the Chancellor, EVC and other senior administrators ought to provide the Faculty Assembly, or its Executive Committee when actually in existence, timely notice of such matters and provide an opportunity to contribute to the matter in its development phase. Typically, draft policies are communicated to the Faculty Assembly Agenda Committee, which takes appropriate measures leading to the formulation of Faculty opinion in accordance with the self-governance procedures described in section 4 of these bylaws. The Faculty recognize that this will not always be possible due to emergencies, legal restrictions or considerations, or other University policy or procedure.
