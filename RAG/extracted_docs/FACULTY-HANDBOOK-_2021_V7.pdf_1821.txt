V.1. Reporting a Violation of Policy or Concerns about Possible Misconduct If a faculty member witnesses or is asked to participate in actions that are not consistent with DKU’s values, policies, standards or laws, he/she may ask his/her unit head or the VCAA for advice. If that is not possible, he/she may contact dku-compliance@dukekunshan.edu.cn to report a concern. Faculty may also directly contact the Office of Human Resources or the Office of Audit and Internal Compliance. These resources are available during normal business hours for confidential advice and consultation.
