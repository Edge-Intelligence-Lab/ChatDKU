The Office will provide both guidance and resources to students and alumni that will help them to explore different job and career opportunities as well as help them to gain important skills, knowledge, and experience in order to be competitive and successful in achieving their long-term career goals. The Career Services Office encourages students to make the most of their Duke Kunshan University experience by accessing relevant campus resources, developing career interests and values, and establishing and maintaining relationships with their peers as well as faculty, staff and alumni.
