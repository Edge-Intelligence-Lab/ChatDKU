Duke Kunshan University aims to attract a high-caliber, diverse, and talented body of students with 50-65% of the student population from China, and 35-50% of students from the rest of the world. The admission criteria will be similar to those at Duke University. While academic excellence and accomplishments broadly defined are important factors for admissions consideration, we also place a high value on the demonstrated desire and drive to succeed in life and also how a student overcomes obstacles in various forms to achieve his or her goals.
