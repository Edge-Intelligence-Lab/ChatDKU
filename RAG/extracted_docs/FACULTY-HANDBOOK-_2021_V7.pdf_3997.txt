Principal investigators may hire grant managers supported by the research fund or operations fund to manage full-time or part-time research grants. Every research project shall have one grant manager to manage and monitor the project’s operations and finance, while a grant manager may manage one or several research projects led by one principal investigator. One grant manager may manage research projects led by different principal investigators after such principal investigators agree such management.
