6.06 Timeliness In order to maintain and support a community that is respectful and free from discrimination, harassment, and related misconduct and to maximize DKU’s ability to respond promptly and effectively, we urge students to come forward with full-disclosure reports of concerns or with complaints as soon as possible. Anonymous reports, while helpful for reporting purposes, limits DKU in the inability to provide appropriate support and assistance to students and hold accountable those found responsible.
