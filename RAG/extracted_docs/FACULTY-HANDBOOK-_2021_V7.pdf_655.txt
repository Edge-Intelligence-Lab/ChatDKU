It is primarily the responsibility of students to keep parents and guardians informed of their academic standing and progress as well as any difficulties that may affect their performance. Faculty and staff may not release or discuss information regarding grades or academic standing of a student with his/her parents or guardians, unless written permission is provided by the student. The University’s registrar does not release end of term or midterm grade information to parents and guardians without students’ written permission. If a student’s academic standing at the University changes, the Office of the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs may notify parents and guardians in writing. Parents and guardians may also be alerted to emergency and extraordinary situations that may impinge upon a student's well-being.
