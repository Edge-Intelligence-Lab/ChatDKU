Employees, who have work-related problems, disagreements either with co-workers or a supervisor, are encouraged to try to resolve these issues by discussing them frankly with their supervisors. At the same time, the University recognizes that not all problems can be resolved in this manner, and employees may sometimes be reluctant to approach their supervisors. An employee who has a problem is encouraged to report that problem either to the immediate supervisor, the head of the employee’s department, or the Human Resources Office.
