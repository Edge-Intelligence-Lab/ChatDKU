This course covers the history of what is now the United States from pre-Columbian times to 1876. It investigates: the voyages of exploration and colonization, including Native American responses to them; the rise of race slavery and its significance to the American revolution; the war of independence; Anglo-American expansion westwards; slave life and culture; industrialization and economic development in the 19th century; and the civil war, the emancipation of slaves, and
