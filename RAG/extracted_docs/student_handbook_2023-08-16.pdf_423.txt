6.05 Resources and Reporting Options 6.05.01 Emergency Law Enforcement, Medical, and Crisis Response Resources As a priority, DKU encourages all individuals to report discrimination, harassment, or related misconduct that may involve criminal conduct to the local Police Department. This could include sexual violence, relationship violence, stalking, and conduct that could be a hate crime. Local Police can be contacted at 110 (Chinese only) and you may reach the Campus Emergency Hotline at (0512) 3665-7110 for help.
