4.15 Good Samaritan Policy & Reporting Incidents Related to Alcohol and/or other Drugs 4.15.01 Good Samaritan Policy In cases of a medical emergency or crisis, DKU students are expected to care for others in the DKU community by seeking help from appropriate University staff members or other helping professionals. The Good Samaritan Policy is in place to ease concerns and eliminate any reason for hesitation that DKU students may have in seeking help for others should an emergency or crisis arise.
