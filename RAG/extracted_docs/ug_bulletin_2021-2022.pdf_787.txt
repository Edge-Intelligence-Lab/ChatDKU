The Global Leadership Academy is designed to help students become global leaders who can positively impact the campus, Kunshan community, and the world. The Global Leadership Academy was created and rooted in a theoretical framework that helps students understand consciousness of self, congruence, commitment, common purpose, controversy with civility, collaboration, and citizenship.
