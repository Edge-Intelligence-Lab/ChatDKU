Students studying at Duke University during a fall or spring term will pay the same tuition they would have paid for a term at DKU, with the same merit scholarship and/or financial aid. Students will be required to cover all other costs, including housing, board, insurance, travel, and living expenses. It should be noted that such costs are higher than at Duke Kunshan. Currently, the total cost for one regular term at Duke (including housing, dining plan, insurance, and student fees) is estimated at USD $14,300; this does not include tuition, visa, or travel expenses.
