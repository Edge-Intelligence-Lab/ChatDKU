All salary, fringe benefits and college service policies are determined by the Duke Kunshan University Board of Trustees on the recommendations of the Executive Vice Chancellor, the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs and the Chief Financial Officer. Faculty members receive salaries as compensation for their contributions to the University in the form of teaching, research, and university service. Salaries are set on an annual basis, but increases are not automatic. The annual salaries of members of the faculty are normally adjusted for the ensuing academic year each July 1. Salary adjustments reflect a variety of factors, including consideration of the level of activity and quality of individual performance in teaching and research; contributions to the University community; competitive salaries in particular fields; budgetary constraints; cost of living adjustment, and other factors.
