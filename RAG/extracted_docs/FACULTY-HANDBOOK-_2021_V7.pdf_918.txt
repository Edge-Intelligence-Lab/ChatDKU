Faculty may, with prior approval of their center director or unit head and the programs that depend on their teaching (undergraduate, graduate) apply for permission to buy out courses with external funding. They may use any source of funding that is large enough to cover a substantial portion of their salary as long as the terms of that funding allow course buy out. Funding from the Chinese Government is often not permitted to be used for course buy out, according to government regulations. It is the faculty applicant’s responsibility to make sure that they understand the terms and stipulations of their funding before they submit an application for permission to buy out.
