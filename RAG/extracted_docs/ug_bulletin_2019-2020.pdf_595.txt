If the student is dissatisfied with the informal resolution outcome, the student may choose to file a formal complaint with the Dean of Undergraduate Studies. The Dean of Undergraduate Studies will consider complaints based on an alleged violation of an academic rule, policy, or established practice. If the student is dissatisfied with the outcome of the formal resolution process, the student may choose to file an appeal with the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs. The Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs makes the final decision regarding appeal outcomes.
