The interview is a vital research tool that crosses forms of media and academic disciplines. However, it can be difficult to know how to conduct a good interview. How does an interview succeed or fail, and who is its audience? Can the interview be an artform in itself? This course explores interviews in various contexts, including popular culture, to analyze the audience expectations and research implications of interviews. Students will build argumentative writing skills by analyzing interviews. They will also conduct a series of interviews themselves, gaining experience as interviewers and interviewees.
