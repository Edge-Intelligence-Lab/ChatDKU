Playing in small formations, students will be able to explore chamber repertoire of all epochs, with a special focus on modern repertoire. The ensembles will be established by the instructor at the begin of the course. Their repertoire will be chosen by the instructor in consultation with the students involved. The groups will participate to one concert per semester. They may occasionally be invited to perform in the wider Jiangsu area. The ensembles will be coached regularly by the instructor.
