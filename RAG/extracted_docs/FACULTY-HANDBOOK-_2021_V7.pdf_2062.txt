a. Proceedings to dismiss a member of the University faculty who has tenure or whose term appointment has not expired shall be initiated by the EVC only after he or she has made sufficient investigation to determine that reasonable grounds exist. The EVC should ordinarily discuss the matter informally in personal conference with the faculty member in question. If the EVC discusses the matter with other officers of the University and with other members of the faculty, care should be taken to keep in confidence any accusations that may reflect adversely on the faculty member under investigation.
