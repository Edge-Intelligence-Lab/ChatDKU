Introduces students to the fundamentals of computer networks. Focuses on layered architecture of the network protocol stack. Case studies will be drawn from the internet, combined with practical programming exercises. Concepts include the internet architecture, HTTP, DNS, P2P, sockets, TCP/IP, BGP, routing protocols, and wireless/mobile networking and their applications such as how to achieve reliable/secure communications over unreliable/insecure channels, how to find a good path through a network, how to share network resources among competing entities, how to find an object in the network, and how to build network applications.
