Duke Kunshan is governed by an independent Board of Trustees and operates under a set of guiding principles that are consistent with those of world-leading universities. Administrative operations are in the charge of the university leadership team, headed by the Chancellor and the Executive Vice Chancellor. Feng Youmei, Duke Kunshan University Chancellor, is a professor, the former Executive Vice President of Wuhan University and the Vice Chairwoman for the Medical Education Committee of the China Association of Higher Education. She has profound expertise in medical education as well as abundant experience in university administration. Executive Vice Chancellor Denis Simon is a long-time expert on U.S.-China bilateral science and technology relations and a member of the U.S. Experts Group within the U.S.-China Innovation Dialogue.
