The committee recommends a second, formal review of academic structure after 5 years (i.e., 2022). During this transitional period, the undergraduate and graduate programs will function separately in terms of their administrative structures. However, both undergraduate and graduate faculty members are strongly encouraged to actively engage with each other about research, teaching, and graduate and undergraduate research supervision, etc.
