2.04 Residence Halls Many use the word “dorm” to describe the places students live on university campuses since “dormitory” is a word derived from the Latin for “sleep”. However, since DKU provides something far more impactful and meaningful than sleep alone, we use the term residence hall to describe the residence halls. Each hall is supervised by a full-time Residence Life Coordinator (RLC), who also lives in the building. Further, each RLC supervises a team of student workers in various positions to help students develop a sense of community in a safe and vibrant environment. Information about each residence hall can be found online.
