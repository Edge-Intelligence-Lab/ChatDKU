4.14 Gambling Gambling is strictly prohibited. A person is gambling if they operate, play, or bet at any game of chance at which any money, property, or other items of value are bet. Poker nights and casino games are permitted only if no admission is charged, no buy-in is required, and no real money is wagered. Raffles of any kind, including those sponsored by student groups are also prohibited unless they meet the poker nights and casino games permitted conditions above.
