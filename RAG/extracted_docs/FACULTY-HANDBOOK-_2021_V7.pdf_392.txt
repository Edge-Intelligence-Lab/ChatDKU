Regular rank faculty who have full time appointments at either Duke or Wuhan University are eligible to be nominated for an up to 5-year, renewable, secondary faculty appointment at Duke Kunshan University (DKU). Secondary faculty are expected to be actively engaged with research at DKU and are not expected to teach19, advise undergraduates or serve on committees at DKU. Secondary faculty appointments are non-tenure/non-tenure track. The rank of a secondary faculty member is determined by the secondary institution and is no higher than his or her rank (or equivalent rank) in his or her primary institution.
