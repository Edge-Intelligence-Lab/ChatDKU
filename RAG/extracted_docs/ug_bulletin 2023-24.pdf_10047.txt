This course examines the challenges and solutions to regulatory policy of modern technologies, such as data analytics (big data), artificial intelligence, cloud computing, biotechnology, and satellites. The course will first examine national and international policy and regulatory approaches to individual technologies. Then, building on this, it will discuss more generally the reasons for regulating technologies, common features of regulation, important actors, and the role of ethics and self-regulation. The interactive work with students will include group discussions, debates, and a mock policy negotiation.
