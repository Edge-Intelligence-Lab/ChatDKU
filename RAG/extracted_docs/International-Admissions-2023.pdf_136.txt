DKU’s campus is a spacious, modern setting with traditional Chinese elements. Its many aquatic features symbolize the area’s famous water towns. As a LEED-certified campus, it is considered a model of sustainable development. Phase 2 of campus opened in Summer of 2023, which more than doubled the size, featuring a state-of-the-art sports complex, innovative library, and brand-new student residences. dukekunshan.edu.cn/campus-life
