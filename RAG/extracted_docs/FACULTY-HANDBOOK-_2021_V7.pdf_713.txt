At the request of the ORSTT, the Finance Office (FO) assigns restricted fund codes (and sub-codes as needed) to new awards. Based on the award analysis provided by the ORSTT, the FO sets up each award in the financial system and subsequently provides quarterly expense reports to the ORSTT and PIs for review. It sends official financial reports or invoices to sponsors as required by the terms of each award. Only the Finance Office can provide official, certified financial reports or invoices to a sponsor.
