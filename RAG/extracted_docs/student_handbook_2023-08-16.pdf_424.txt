For incidents of sexual violence, sexual exploitation, relationship violence, and stalking, DKU also encourages individuals to seek assistance from a medical provider or crisis response service immediately after an incident. This provides the opportunity to address physical well-being or health concerns, preserve any available evidence, and begin a timely investigative and remedial response. Emotional care, counseling, and crisis response are also available on and off-campus; e.g., at Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS), caps@dukekunshan.edu.cn.
