Populism is widely recognized as a threat to liberal democratic states around the world today. In this course, we will analyze the phenomenon of populism from three basic perspectives. First, we will read theories of what populism is and how it relates to liberal democracy. Second, we will examine what the aims and practices of different populist movements from around the world are (movements such as those in Venezuela, Brazil, Hungary, Poland, Turkey, France, and India). Third, we will investigate what the causes of populism are and how we can respond to it.
