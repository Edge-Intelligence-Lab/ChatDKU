Article 11. Indirect Costs include the costs of facilities and administration generated by research activities and paid for by the University that cannot be directly allocated to a specific sponsored project. The term ‘facilities’ includes equipment and space, water, electricity, gas, heating, cleaning, maintenance and other operational costs provided by DKU for research projects, as well as various operational administrative fees, and the cost of performance improvement. The term “Administration” refers to the costs of daily operation of the University’s research administration in organizing and managing projects and other related administrative costs.
