Student Residence Hall: The Student Residence Hall will primarily house graduate students and some undergraduate students. Students are assigned to a four-person apartment suite with either 4 single rooms or 2 single rooms and 1 double room. Each suite has two bathrooms. A typical suite has a living room, four private single bedrooms (or one two-bed bedroom and two single bedrooms) and two bathrooms with showers. Amenities in a suite include study desk, chair, lamp, single bed (or two single beds in double rooms), closets, TV, kettle, waste baskets, cleaning tools, and drinking water faucet. All undergraduate students are required to live in on-campus housing. Graduate students may live off-campus after approval from Residence Life.
