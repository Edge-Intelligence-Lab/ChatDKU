The course leads to an examination of the experience of holocaust, the quest for Jewish statehood and its impact upon the political situation in the Middle East. In so doing the course develops broad theoretical approaches in religious studies that can serve wider interests in understanding the relationship between nation, state, ethnicity, and religious belonging. Other religious contexts may be introduced by way of comparison.
