A. For all inventions or Supportive Technology assigned to the University under this Policy, the University will at no expense to the inventor make reasonable efforts to evaluate the interest to others in commercializing the invention, seek licenses and options for licenses, have applications for patents filed and prosecuted, and otherwise manage the inventions and Supportive Technology or arrange for their management by recognized patent management organizations. The University may assign such inventions or Supportive Technology to a foundation, trust, corporation or other entity organized by the University for purposes of patent / rights management.
