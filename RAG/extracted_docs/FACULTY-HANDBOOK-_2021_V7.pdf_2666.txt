While the DKPN provides a reasonable environment for security, it will not meet every type of condition and restriction that can be commonly found in research contracts. The DKPN will address many, including country of data domicile (PRC), restricted physical access to systems (data center), management of end user systems to install updates and ensure security, secure password environment, restricted access to data store, separation of servers holding confidential data, logging of access rights, and IT access approval process.
