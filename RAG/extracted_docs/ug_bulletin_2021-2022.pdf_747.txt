Eligible students will have the opportunity to attend Duke University in Durham, North Carolina, in either the fall or spring term of their junior year. The Office of Global Education will take into account individual timing (fall/spring term) preferences. However, students may not be admitted to preferred terms due to capacity limitations. Students will also have the option to attend Duke Summer Session. Normally, students would be encouraged to take the summer session either immediately before or after their junior-year term assignment.
