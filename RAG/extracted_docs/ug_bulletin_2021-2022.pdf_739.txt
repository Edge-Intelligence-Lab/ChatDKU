Students must apply and receive approval to participate in any credit-bearing study away opportunities through the Office of Global Education. Approved programs (either on DKU’s preapproved program list, or approved by individual petition) will be eligible for credit transfer back to DKU. All students who plan to study away must apply through DKU, receive approval to participate, and sign the DKU Participation Agreement before committing or accepting enrollment in any credit-bearing program.
