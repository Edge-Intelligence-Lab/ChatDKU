Counseling is an important part of the Duke Kunshan University community and it supports both systemic and individual needs. It helps to create a more supportive community and protocols are in place to help guide actions to handle crisis situations including suicidal attempts and sexual assaults. It also helps individuals to handle mental issues such as anxiety, depression and adjustment disorders. More importantly, CAPS provides services to help student grow in more confident and mature manner, and aims to create a healthy community through various workshops on time-management, stress reduction through mindfulness-based stress reduction techniques.
