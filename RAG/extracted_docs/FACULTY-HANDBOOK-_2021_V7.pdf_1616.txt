4.2.5.2 The Curriculum Committee is the Faculty’s representative body for collaboration with the University administration and Duke University Faculty’s efforts to create, revise, and implement the University’s curriculum. Curriculum Committee membership includes: five elected Faculty members, representing a minimum of five academic divisions. The Associate Vice-Chancellor for Undergraduate Programs, the Associate Dean of Undergraduate Curricular Affairs, the Associate Vice-Chancellor for Graduate Studies and Research, and the Registrar shall be ex officio members.
