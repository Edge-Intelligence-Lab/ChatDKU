The Dining Customer Relations Team (CDCRT) aims at improving campus dining services to meet the diverse needs of Duke Kunshan University customers, including students, staff and faculty, and building a positive dialogue between the customers and the service provider. The team meets monthly to provide constructive input on issues of food services and collectively prioritize and propose solutions to help improve the situation. Collaborating with campus food services, Student Affairs team organized various activities to facilitate cross-cultural communication and to establish a community on campus, including Mid-autumn moon cake making, Thanksgiving Dinner, cookie baking, Dragon Boat Festival wrapping “zong zi”, and dumpling making, etc.
