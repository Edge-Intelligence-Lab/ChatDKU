Tinglin Park 亭林公园 Tinglin Park, home of the Kunqu Opera, is situated at the east foot of Ma'an Mountain (also named Kunshan Mountain) in Kunshan City. There are many attractions located within the park, such as a zoo, an amusement park, a bonsai garden and a Buddhist temple. No.1, Ma An Shan East Road 马鞍山东路1 号
