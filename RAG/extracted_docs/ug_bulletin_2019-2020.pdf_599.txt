A student who questions a final grade received in a course should first discuss the matter with the instructor(s) within thirty days of the grade being released. After meeting with the instructor(s), if the student still believes the instructor(s) has assigned an inaccurate or unjustified grade, the student may make a formal complaint to the appropriate Division Chair, or to the Director of the Language and Culture Center for language related courses. The formal complaint must be submitted prior to the first day of classes for the semester immediately following the recording of the grade.
