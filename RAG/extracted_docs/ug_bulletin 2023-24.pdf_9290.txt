Calculus is the foundation for a large part of modern mathematics and has countless applications across the sciences and beyond. This course covers the fundamental Calculus concepts (limits, continuity, differentiation, integration) and explores related applications. The treatment of these concepts assumes some prior knowledge of Calculus. Recommended for students who have had a previous (high-school level) Calculus course. Students who have not had such a Calculus course are recommended to take MATH 101 instead.
