D. Inventions or Supportive Technology arising from research financed by the Chinese Government are controlled by the terms of the applicable grant or contract and the provisions of PRC law. The University is obliged to report to the appropriate government agency all such inventions, discoveries or Supportive Technology for definition of the government’s rights and interests. In cases where the government claims no patent rights or waives its rights, such inventions or Supportive Technology shall fall under Article V, section C, immediately above, subject to such limitations as the government may impose.
