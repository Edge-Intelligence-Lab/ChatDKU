Summer Terms are structured for first-year Chinese students to take courses to meet the MOE requirements and for junior or senior students to take certain courses to meet requirements towards graduation. First-year students who wish to delay taking the required Chinese courses must submit a petition form to the Office of the Registrar. All course changes need to be approved by the student’s advisor and processed by the Office of the Registrar. The deadlines for requesting withdrawals from a course also apply to the seven-week long summer term.
