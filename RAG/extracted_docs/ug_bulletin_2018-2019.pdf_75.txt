Wuhan University is one of the top ten comprehensive universities in China with over 120 years of history. It has 34 schools and colleges which offer 122 undergraduate programs, 44 master programs and 44 PhD programs. By March 2018, Wuhan University's student body consists of 29,738 full-time undergraduates and 18,817 full-time master's degree candidates, 7,256 Ph.D. candidates and 2,265 international students. It has 3,948 faculty in 2018 with 1,513 full professors, 2,376 associate professors, 11 Academicians from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, 7 Academicians from Chinese Academy of Engineering, 10 Senior Professors in Liberal Arts and Human Sciences and 15 Nationally Renowned Teachers.
