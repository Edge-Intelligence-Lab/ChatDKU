The course will also draw upon research on leadership, cultural values, and norms to systematically perceptions of leadership. Applications explored will include religion-based portrayals of gender, race ethnicity, and age in assessing leaders. Also considered will be whether these portrayals make specific religious lenses more or less amenable to leadership development in different contexts (for example where innovation is important or where co-leadership is essential).
