The Dean will review the case and decide whether there are grounds to convene a faculty committee to review the grade. If the Dean decides there are no grounds, then the grade is not changed. If the Dean decides that there are grounds to proceed, the case will be referred to a committee consisting of the faculty members of the Undergraduate Academic Review Board. This committee will then evaluate and review the case, and the Dean may initiate a grade change if that is the recommendation of the committee. The decision of the committee is final.
