Duke has 3,637 faculty. In 2017, Clarivate Analytics listing of the world’s most-cited researchers included 34 names from Duke out of 3,400 researchers from 21 fields of science and social science. Duke also ranks among the top national universities to have produced Rhodes, Marshall, Truman, Goldwater, and Udall Scholars. Ten Nobel laureates and three Turing Award winners are affiliated with the university.
