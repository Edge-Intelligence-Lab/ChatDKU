Consensual Relationships: romantic or sexual relationships willingly undertaken by the parties. It is recognized that in faculty-student relationships the power dynamic is inherently unequal, and the willingness of a student to enter such a relationship may be driven in part by pressure from the more powerful faculty member. However, for the purposes of this policy, any relationship which is presented by the consenting parties as consensual will be treated as such. (This policy does not apply to cases where one party accuses the other of coercion.)
