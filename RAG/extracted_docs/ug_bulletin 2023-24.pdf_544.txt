The grade for signature work is reflected in the grades issued for the two capstone courses and includes the SW project proposal, final public presentation, experiential learning, e(DKU)-portfolio and final SW product. Grading of the two capstone courses will be based on the academic letter grade system (see section on Grading and Grade Requirements). Students who do not successfully pass signature work and all the required components will be required to repeat failed capstone courses and requirements, resubmit the final SW product, and complete any missing signature work requirements the next academic year.
