Any violation of this policy shall be deemed misconduct as that term is used in the Faculty Handbook. Violation of the policy may result in sanctions for the faculty member, including but not limited to, mandatory training or counseling, reprimand, probation, suspension, loss of privileges, demotion, removal of title(s), or termination. The Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs, in consultation with faculty member’s immediate administrative supervisor (e.g., program director, center director, dean), shall determine sanctions for violations. The faculty member may appeal the decision on sanction to the Executive Vice Chancellor.
