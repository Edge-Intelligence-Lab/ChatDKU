If the relevant parties are not satisfied with the decision of the EVC they may submit a complaint and corresponding evidence to the EVC within 15 days of receipt of the VCAA’s written notification. The VCAA appoints the Faculty Hearing Committee or an ad hoc Hearing Committee in cases outside of the jurisdiction of the Faculty Hearing Committee, which committees will begin their investigation immediately. The Faculty Hearing Committee’s composition and jurisdiction are described in the Duke Kunshan University Policy on Academic Tenure and Faculty Appointment, Promotion and Tenure 94.
