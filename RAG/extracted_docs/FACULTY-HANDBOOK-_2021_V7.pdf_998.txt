The University is committed to providing a work environment that is free from all forms of unlawful harassment. The University strictly prohibits unlawful harassment of any kind, including harassment on the basis of sex (including pregnancy, childbirth or related medical condition), race, color, religion, gender, age, mental or physical disability, legally protected medical condition, national origin, marital status, veteran status, sexual orientation, or any other legally protected characteristic or condition. This policy covers all forms of harassment by, among and/or between employees, customers, contractors, vendors or other university occupants.
