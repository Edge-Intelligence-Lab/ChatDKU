For a student enrolled in 16 or more credits, the following grades will result in academic probation for the succeeding spring or fall term: during the first term of the freshman year, grades including DD, DF, or FF; during any subsequent spring/fall term, grades including DDD, DF or FF (as long as the student has passed at least 12 credits); and during two consecutive spring/fall terms, grades including DDDD, DDDF, or DDFF.
