Passing grades are A, exceptional; B, superior; C, satisfactory; CR, pass (see Credit/No Credit grading system below); and D, low pass. These grades (except CR) may be modified by a plus or minus. A Z grade is assigned for the satisfactory completion of CAPSTONE 496 when it is taken in the one term, but the student’s final public presentation is scheduled for the following term. The final grade is assigned when all the signature work requirements are completed. The Z grade must be resolved according to the Signature Work timeline of the subsequent semester. If the Z is not completed by the deadline, it will convert to an F.
