Why does music affect our emotions so strongly? Why do we like (or not like) something we hear, and what do we like about it? During this course, we will try to answer these and other fascinating questions about music. We will review the basic structures of music and study several genres and styles, ranging from masterpieces of classical music to rock and EDM (Electronic Dance Music). We will also investigate the cultural conditions under which we listen. We will develop the understanding of those matters primarily though written commentary on the musical and scholarly material encountered. No prior musical knowledge is required.
