Adherence to this Policy as amended from time to time shall be deemed to be a condition of initial or continuing employment of every university employee and a condition of enrollment and attendance of every student or researcher who works on any research project under university control. All such employees, students and researchers will be expected to sign agreements incorporating the terms of this Policy; but failure to sign shall not affect the applicability of the Policy nor relieve any employee, student or researcher from the obligations imposed by it.
