4.2.5.3 The Faculty Development Committee makes recommendations concerning ways to improve the Faculty’s ability to advance as productive scholars; the committee sponsors workshops and other programs to aid faculty members in their professional development. It recommends the avenues for dispersion of the faculty development budget and assists in the review of applications for pre-tenure sabbatical. Faculty Development Committee membership includes: five elected Faculty members from a minimum of five academic divisions and the Director of Faculty Affairs (ex officio).
