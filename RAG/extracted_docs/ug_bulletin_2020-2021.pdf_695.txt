Students studying at Duke University during the semester will pay the same tuition they would have paid for a semester at DKU, with the same merit scholarship and/or financial aid. Students will be required to cover all other costs, including housing, board, insurance, travel, and living expenses. It should be noted that such costs are higher than at Duke Kunshan. Currently we calculate these incremental costs for one semester to be approximately USD 7,500 for international students and RMB 54,000 for Chinese students, excluding flights.
