Instructor – entry level; MA, MSc or equivalent, some teaching experience (TA or other). May be promoted to Lecturer after one term. Duties include: Predominantly teaching, with some service. Lecturer – Ph.D., MA, MSc or equivalent + several years of college-level teaching experience. May be promoted to Senior Lecturer after two terms. Candidates with a Ph.D. may be considered for an earlier promotion review, depending on their experience and record of contribution to educational programs or to the field of education. Duties include: teaching and service to the University. On-going professional development is expected, as is participation in and service to the profession.
