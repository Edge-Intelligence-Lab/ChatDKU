Inasmuch as a publication or public disclosure prior to the filing of a Chinese patent application is a bar to the grant of certain foreign patents and can bar the grant of a Chinese patent if it occurred six months earlier than the filing date, it may be necessary in some circumstances to temporarily restrict publication for short periods of time. Accordingly, the University may request employees to delay disclosure or the publication date of any publication which discloses an invention or Supportive Technology falling under Article V
