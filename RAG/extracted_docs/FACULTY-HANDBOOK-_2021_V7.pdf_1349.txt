Luzhi 甪直 Luzhi is another old but extremely beautiful water town in Kunshan. With a history of more than 2,500 years, it compares favorably to Zhouzhuang Water Town. Covering about 247 acres (100 hectares). The natural beauty of the town is indescribable. Old stone bridges, limpid water, maidenhair trees and old-style dwellings. In addition, the town is noted for several historical and cultural relics, such as Baosheng temple, the Wansheng Rice Company and the White Lotus Flower Temple built in the Northern Song Dynasty (960-1279). It’s not only famous as a water town, but also well-known for women’s clothing, in the style of the Han nationality’s dress of China. Transport: ~35 minutes by taxi.
