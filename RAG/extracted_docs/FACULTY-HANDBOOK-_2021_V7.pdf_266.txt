I.1. According to the DKU policy on Academic Tenure and Faculty Appointment Promotion and Tenure, a faculty member (applicant) may apply for an extension of his/her pre-third year review period or pre-tenure review period of up to 1 year (either 1 full semester or two full semesters) due to an exceptional circumstance or life event (other than the COVID-19 pandemic). Requests for all such extensions shall be made in writing to the head of the academic unit as a first step and forwarded to the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs (VCAA) for final approval within 3 months of the onset of the life event7.
