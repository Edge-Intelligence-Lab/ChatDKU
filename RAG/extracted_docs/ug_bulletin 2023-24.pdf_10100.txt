Christians around the world worship Jesus of Nazareth, whom they refer to as the Messiah or Christ. But who was this person? With what level of accuracy can we retrace the historical circumstances of his life, preaching, and death? How did this Jewish preacher perceive himself and his mission? What was the message that he preached? And how did his early followers understand that message? This course aims to address these and other questions relating to the historical figure of Jesus in the historical-cultural context of 1st century Palestine.
