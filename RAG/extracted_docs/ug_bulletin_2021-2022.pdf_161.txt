The distributional requirement is intended to broaden students’ educational experience and ensure that all graduates have an acquaintance with a broad variety of fields of inquiry and approaches to knowledge. Students are required to take at least one four-credit course 2 in each of the three divisional areas: arts and humanities, natural and applied sciences, and social sciences. Courses that fulfill the distribution requirement are designated by divisional area attributes. The distribution requirement cannot be fulfilled with AP or IPC credits.
