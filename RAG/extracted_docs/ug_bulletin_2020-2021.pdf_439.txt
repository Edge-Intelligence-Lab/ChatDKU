Approval forms for Duke Kunshan University students taking courses at institutions other than Duke Kunshan University and Duke University may be obtained online or from the Office of Global Education. Students wishing to transfer credit for study at another accredited college or university while on leave, on a DKU-approved study abroad program, or during the summer must present a catalog of that college or university to the Director of Global Education and the corresponding division chair or Director of the Language and Culture Center and obtain their approval prior to taking the courses. No credit will be accepted for coursework taken while a Duke Kunshan University student is withdrawn, suspended, or on involuntary leave of absence.
