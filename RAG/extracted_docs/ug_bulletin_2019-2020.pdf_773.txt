The expertise of mathematics and computational sciences is in dire need, as access to unprecedented amounts of is creating new opportunities, working together in multidisciplinary teams, to actively engage with and to change the world around them. Mathematics and computing sciences are extensively applied in economics, biology, medical science as well as quantitative social science including global health, environmental science and humanities (for example, digital media). New application areas are constantly being discovered and established techniques are being applied in new ways and in emerging fields. Consequently, a wide variety of career opportunities are open to people with mathematical and computational talent and training.
