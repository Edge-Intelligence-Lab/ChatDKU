Duke Kunshan University (“DKU” or the “University”) Undergraduate Program expects that students obtain textbooks (including, but not limited to, printed books, eBooks, and software) through legal channels. For convenience of students, the Learning Resources Management at DKU Library is assigned to purchase and deliver required textbooks to students prior to the beginning of each session. The textbook fee is charged to the student account and deducted from the student’s direct deposit.
