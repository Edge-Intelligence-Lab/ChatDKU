In recognition that observances of religious holidays may affect classroom attendance and the submission of graded work, members of the various religious affiliations should inform their instructors of major religious holidays that occur when classes are being held at or before the beginning of each term. In the event of a conflict, students wishing to observe a specific religious holiday should request that their instructors arrange for a postponement or makeup of work.
