All directors of graduate studies report to the Director of Graduate Programs and Recruitment, and also report to the relevant program/institute director if applicable. The Director of Graduate Programs and Recruitment and program/institute directors report to the VCAA. The performance of all directors of graduate studies should be evaluated by the corresponding program/institute director. If graduate programs grow substantially in the future (including the possibility of PhD programs), it is recommended to appoint an Associate Dean of Graduate Programs and Recruitment. The Associate Dean of Graduate Programs and Recruitment should report to the VCAA and will provide strategic oversight and leadership for all DKU graduate programs.
