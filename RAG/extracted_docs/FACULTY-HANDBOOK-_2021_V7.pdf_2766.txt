A financial conflict of interest (FCOI) occurs when an individual’s private interests and his or her obligations to the University converge to raise reasonable concerns of bias to an independent observer. Therefore, in order to manage situations where a potential for an FCOI arises, this Policy is intended to assist Duke Kunshan researchers in applying and complying with the requirements of Duke Kunshan regarding the disclosure, review and reporting of FCOI in research and with the requirements of the sponsors of Duke Kunshan.
