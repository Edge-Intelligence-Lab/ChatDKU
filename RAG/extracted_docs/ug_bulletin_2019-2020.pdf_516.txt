The change of grading basis to CR/NC can be done through filing a form with the registrar, but students need to get approval from their advisors. Students who receive a NC (by changing a grade of D+ or below) should be mindful of continuation requirements, e.g. that they may be subject to academic suspension (see section on Continuation), or that they may have to take an extra course the following semester or during the summer in order to ensure that they graduate on time.
