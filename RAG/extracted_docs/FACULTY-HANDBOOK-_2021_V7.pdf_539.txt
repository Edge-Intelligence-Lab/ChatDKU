The successful conduct of a course depends upon a spirit of mutual respect and cooperation among its participants. If a student disrupts a class in such a way that it seriously compromises the educational experience of the course for other students and/or prevents the instructor from accomplishing the goals of the course as outlined in the syllabus, the instructor may require the student to leave the class meeting. In the case of undergraduate students, the Dean of Undergraduate Studies will be notified of this action. In the case of Graduate students, the Associate Dean for Graduate Programs should be notified.
