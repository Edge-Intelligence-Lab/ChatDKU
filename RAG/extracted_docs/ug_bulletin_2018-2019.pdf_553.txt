The mission of Student Affairs at Duke Kunshan University is to cultivate students to be global citizens through participatory leadership, cross-cultural and experiential learning. Programs and Services are planned to support the intellectual, social, cultural development and wellness of students. The Student Affairs staff team, overseen by the Associate Dean for Student Affairs, aims to provide programs and services that enhance and complement students’ academic experience by providing opportunities for students to explore various services and interests beyond the classroom. These opportunities include workshops and services provided by the Campus Health and Psychological Counseling departments, and programs provided by the Residential Life staff and Resident Assistants as well as Graduate Resident Fellows. Campus- wide programs and recreational events provided by our Student Activities Officer include “Orienteering Fun Run” during Orientation Weeks to encourage team work and “getting-to- know” each other. Activities such as the 5K run, bike tours, various intra-mural sports, fitness exercises, meditation are aimed to enhance health awareness and promote healthy living. Student clubs and organizations are available to promote student governance and encourage student leadership development. Local and regional cultural excursions and arts and cultural workshops such as cross-cultural communication, tea culture, painting, and pottery making are aimed to heighten student esthetic appreciation and cultural competencies.
