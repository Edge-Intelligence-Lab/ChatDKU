WOC 212 continues to prepare students for their signature work at DKU and similar projects. This course will guide students in conducting their proposed research and developing a preliminary research paper. Students will do writing assignments in which they analyze data, assess data's validity and reliability, report findings based on research questions, and discuss their findings in light of other relevant studies.
