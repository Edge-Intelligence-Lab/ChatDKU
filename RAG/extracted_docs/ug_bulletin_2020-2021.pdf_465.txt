Only in exceptional cases and with special permission of both the academic advisor and the Dean of Undergraduate Studies, could a student enroll in an overload (defined as more than 10 credits in a 7-week session or more than 20 credits in a semester, exclusive of PE), up to a maximum of 24 credits. In determining whether to approve an overload, the academic advisor and the Dean of Undergraduate Studies will carefully consider the student’s academic history, including grades and the number of courses taken in the past.
