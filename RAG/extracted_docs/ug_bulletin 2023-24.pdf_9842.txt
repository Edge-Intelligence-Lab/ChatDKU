This course will provide a comprehensive overview of the Chinese economy and China’s role in the world economy. China’s current economic challenges will be given particular attention. Topics that will be addressed include: the Chinese economy before 1949; the socialist era, 1949-1978; economic reform and market transition; the role of state enterprises; foreign investment; foreign trade; China’s role in the East Asian trade-production network; the Chinese financial system; imbalances; the Chinese monetary and exchange rate policy; China’s role internationalization of the Yuan; and the housing market.
