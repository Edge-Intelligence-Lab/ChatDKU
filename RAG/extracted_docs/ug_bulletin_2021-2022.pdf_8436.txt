WOC 211 aims at preparing students for their signature work at DKU and similar projects. This research preparedness course will engage students with writing on the research process and guide them in producing a research proposal on their chosen topics. Students will search for and evaluate relevant literature, store and manage references, frame the research question(s), and determine research approaches. Students will develop their critical thinking, problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills during the research proposal writing process. These learning modules will help students learn how to engage in academic conversations and contribute to the existing literature.
