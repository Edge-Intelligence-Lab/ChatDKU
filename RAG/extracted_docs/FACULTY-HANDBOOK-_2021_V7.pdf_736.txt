As stewards of Duke Kunshan University’s resources, all faculty, researchers, staff, and students are expected to exercise sound judgment to collect and use research data prudently and ethically. Additionally, various foreign, national, and provincial laws impose obligations on Duke Kunshan. Grants and contracts may impose requirements for the protection and preservation of associated research data. Special consideration to research data is warranted, as some research data may be classified as public and open, while other research data may require greater protections due to the sensitivity of the data.
