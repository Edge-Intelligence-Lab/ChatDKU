The Blue Oasis – DKU Student Center (BOSC) is a multi-functioned student centered space located by the lake at the Dayu Bay commercial street within less than 10-minute walk from the West Gate of the university. BOSC is a two-floor complex with different room setups to accommodate various types of events and activities. We welcome students to propose ideas and activities to support in implement them. Whether you want to study, hang out with your friends, or just come have fun at BOSC activities, this is the place to be! BOSC is staffed by student workers and is overseen by the Office of Campus Engagement.
